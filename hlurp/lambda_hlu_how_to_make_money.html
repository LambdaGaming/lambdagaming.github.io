This guide will explain how to make money throughout the different RPs on the server, as well as what you can spend the money on.

BMRP:Cascade

 Ways To Make Money:

  • Escaping the facility.
  • Escorting a medic out of the facility as HECU.
  • Killing players that are on opposing teams.
  • Killing NPCs. (Zombie and vortigaunt players count as opposing teams and not NPCs.)
  • Extinguishing fires.

 What To Spend Money On:

  • Only the items in your F4 menu, unless you buy weapons from players or pay to not be killed.


BMRP:Classic

 Ways To Make Money:

  • Working on major projects that would prompt the administrator to give you a reward for your hard work.
  • Selling weapons as a weapons specialist.
  • Killing NPCs from Xen, events, or the cascade.
  • Extinguishing fires as a service official.
  • Putting trash bags in the trash compactor as a service official.
  • Fixing broken doors as a service official. (Event)
  • Fixing the portal as a service official. (Event)
  • Reviving dead NPCs as a medic. (Event)
  • Cleaning up biohazard spills as a biochemist. (Event)
  • Returning lost crystals to the portal as a survey member. (Event)
  • Potentially selling any loot or specimens you may find in Xen as a survey member.
  • Fixing the server as a technician. (Event)

 What To Spend Money On:

  • Paying fines as administrator to the government man NPC. (Event)
  • Rewarding players as administrator for working on a project, invention, etc.
  • Buying weapons off of the weapons specialist.
  • F4 menu items such as TVs, lab equipment, personal alarm systems, and computer terminals.


City 17/City 24 RP

 Ways To Make Money:

  • Selling ammo and armor to refugees as a black market dealer.
  • Grinding your rocks in the citadel for a profit.
  • Selling crafted weapons or raw iron to the Combine as a loyal citizen.
  • Killing zombies or headcrabs. (Events)

 What To Spend Money On:

  • Buying ammo and armor from the black market dealer.
  • F4 menu items such as TVs, crafting tables, and mining equipment.


Outland RP

 Ways To Make Money:

  • None, unless rebels want to charge each other for vehicles, crafting items, weapons, etc, which isn't recommended.

 What To Spend Money On:

  • Airwatch and Groundwatch vehicles. (Combine only)
  • F4 menu items such as TVs, crafting tables, and medical supplies.
  • Turret defense systems. (Rebels only)
