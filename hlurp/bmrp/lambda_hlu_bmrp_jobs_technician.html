Black Mesa RP Job: Technician

In-Game Description:

As a technician, your job is to work on all types of computer systems. You can also help other scientists if they have a question about Wiremod or GTerminal. Recommended for advanced Wiremod users.


Duties:

  • Build servers using GTerminal and PCMod.

  • Build and set up computer systems for other players who need one.

  • Build advanced wiremod creations that could highly benefit the facility.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Ability to use certain Wiremod and PCMod tools that no other jobs have.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • Don't abuse your ability to use advanced wire creations. Don't annoy, kill, teleport, or trap people with it.
