Black Mesa RP Job: Service Official

In-Game Description:

As a Service Official, your job is to clean up the facility by painting over spills/stains, inspecting various machines, extinguishing fires, and cleaning up stray props. This is the only job that has unlimited access to maintenence areas around the facility. You are equipped with a fire extinguisher in case things get out of hand. If a fire does break out, contain it. If you see a fire that is spreading, call in for more service personnel help you contain the fire. You are also required to report any fires to the safety advisor or administrator.


Duties:

  • Blow loose trash into a pile with the leaf blower, then you can put the trash in a dumpster. Once the dumpster is full, you can take it to a seprate location and empty the dumpster.

  • Extinguish any fires that may break out.

  • Tidy up the facility by putting crates back in order, putting wet floor signs down if needed, etc.

  • Use the paint tool to cover up stains that may appear on the map.

  • Inspect anyhing that you are asked to.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has access to a fire extinguisher.

  • Has access to maintenance hallway connecting Sector A to Sector C.

  • Has access to most of the facility. (Can't go to military base or hazardous areas without escort.)

  • Has access to the leaf blower, to put large amounts of trash into a pile to throw away.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • Don't abuse or spam the leaf blower. Only throw away what you know is trash.

  • Don't spam the fire extinguisher. Only use it if you see a fire.

  • Breaking these rules could result in a job ban.
