Black Mesa RP Job: Cook

In-Game Description:

As a cook, it is your job to make sure all personnel are well fed at all times. There are certain locations in the facility reserved specifically for you to set up a cooking station. This job is set up so people can still eat, even when you are not around. For example, if you are setting up a cafe in Sector C, but the Survey Team wants some food in Sector A, you can place a microwave in Sector A, and automatically get money every time someone gets food from it. You can also use non-microwave food that can be bought from the F4 menu and sold to customers.


Duties:

  • Serve food to hungry personnel.

  • Set up a cafe at one of the reserved locations.

  • Set up a microwave if someone requests one be placed near them.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • You have the ability to spawn food.

  • You have the ability to spawn a microwave.

  • You have reserved spots on the map just for making a cafe.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • Set up a microwave where you know a lot of people are. Don't just set one up for one person only.

  • Only use the reserved spots for setting up a cafe.

  • Don't leave food lying around. Remove food yourelf or call a service offical to take it away for you.
