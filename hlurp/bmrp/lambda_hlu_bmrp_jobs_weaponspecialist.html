Black Mesa RP Job: Weapons Specialist

In-Game Description:

As a Weapons Specialist, you are in charge of all weapons at Black Mesa. You are to supply your fellow marines as well as the Black Mesa Security Force with weapons. You can also request a custom weapon to be made by the weapons engineer, such as a self-destructing ground unit, or a drone with a mounted weapon.


Duties:

  • Supply weapons and ammo to Marines and Security.

  • Ask the Weapons Engineer to build custom weapons that will benefit your fellow Marines and Security Officers.

  • Join your fellow Marines to contain Black Mesa in the event of an emergency.

  • Help train future Marines and/or security officers using the on-site military training course.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has access to all official weapons used throughout Black Mesa.

  • Has ability to request custom weapons be built by the Weapons Engineer.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • You must only give appropriate weapons to certain jobs. Basically, don't give an M4 to a security officer, a FiveSeven to a marine, etc. Most weapons are sorted by class: either HECU or Security. Breaking this rule could result in a job ban.
