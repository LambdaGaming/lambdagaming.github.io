Black Mesa RP Job: Biochemist

In-Game Description:

Your main job is to study the crystals the Survey Team brings back from Xen. If you have the time, you can study other biological and living things. You can study living things by using your NPC spawner tool. This tool is known throughout Black Mesa as a mobile-mini-dimensional-portal. It allows you to spawn any NPC in the game. Don't abuse it by spamming the NPC count though; you may be banned from this job for doing so.


Duties:

  • Study crystals brought back from Xen by the Survey Team. You can take notes on the color, shape, width, length, thickness, and radiation levels of each crystal.

  • Take crystals to AMS control room for testing after you are done researching them.

  • Study organic matter, such as aliens and plants from Xen.

Special Job-Specific Items/Tips:

  • Ability to spawn and craft various alien NPCs as well as work in the Sector C Bio Labs, which are restricted to most jobs

  • Has access to the biochemist crafting table.
