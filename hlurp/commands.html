Facility Administrator

"!alarm" Remotely activates the facility alarm system.

"!announce" Initializer for vox commands. Does nothing on its own. Below is a list of available vox announcements that can be used as arguments.

  • damage "Any damage control team to Sector C immediately."
  • evacuate "Administration personnel evacuate Sector D immediately."
  • report "Attention. Any Sector C science personnel please report status."
  • reportviolation "Attention. Report any security violation to administration sub level one."
  • scientistevacuate "Attention. Any Sector C science personnel evacuate area immediately."
  • security "Attention. Security personnel to Sector D."
  • biohazard "Biohazard warning in Sector B and C."
  • inspection "Inspection team to radioactive materials handling bay."
  • hallaccess "Maintenance area access granted."
  • reportams "Sector C science personnel report to anomalous materials test lab."
  • mendown "Security officer reports men down in Sector C."
  • checkelevator "Service team check elevator one Sector C."
  • tram "Transportation control reports all systems on time."
  • energy "Warning. Anomalous energy field detected in administration center."
  • detonations "Warning. High energy detonation detected in materials lab."
  • elevator "Warning. Sector C elevator failure do not use."

Earth's Administrator

"!announce" Initializer for overwatch commands. Does nothing on its own. Below is a list of available vox announcements that can be used as arguments.

  • "person" "Attention please: unidentified person of interest - confirm your civil status with local protection team immediately."
  • "anticiv" "Protection team alert: evidence of anti-civil activity in this community: code assemble, clamp, contain."
  • "evadeciv" "Attention please: Evasion behavior consistent with mal-compliant defendant. Ground Protection Team: alert, code: isolate, expose, administer."
  • "civreport" "Citizen reminder: inaction is conspiracy. Report counter-behavior to a Civil Protection team immediately."
  • "position" "Attention please: All citizens in local residential block, assume your inspection positions."
  • "failure" "Attention ground units. Mission failure will result in permanent offworld assignment. Code reminder: sacrifice, coagulate, clamp."
  • "capitalmal" "Individual, you are charged with capital malcompliance. Anti-citizen status approved."
  • "socifive" "Individual, you are now charged with Socio-Endangerment, level 5. Cease evasion immediately, receive your verdict."
  • "violation" "Individual, you are convicted of multi-anti-civil violations. Implicit citizenship revoked, status: malignant."
  • "unrest" "Alert: Community Ground Protection units: Local unrest structure detected. Assemble, administer, pacify."
  • "evasion" "Attention Protection Team: status evasion in progress in this community. Respond, isolate, inquire."
  • "block" "Attention occupants: your block is now charged with permissive inactive coercion. 5 ration units deducted."
  • "socione" "Individual: you are charged with Socio-endangerment, level 1. Protection Units: prosecution code: duty, sword, midnight."
  • "check" "Citizen notice: priority identification check in progress. Please assemble in your designated inspection positions."
  • "miscount" "Attention residents: miscount detected in your block. Cooperation with your Civil Protection team permits full ration reward."
  • "relocation" "Citizen notice: Failure to cooperate will result in permanent off-world relocation."
  • "exogen" "Alert. Nonstandard Exogen activity detected. Execute containment procedure and report."
