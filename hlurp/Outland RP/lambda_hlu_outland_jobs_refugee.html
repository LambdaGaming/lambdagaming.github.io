In-Game Description:

Refugees are civilians of City 17 (and City 24) who have decided to fight against the combine. During City 17 RP and City 24 RP, refugees are to start the uprising by crafting weapons and fighting the Combine. During Outland RP, refugees have the option to act as guards for rebel bases, but they can also choose to work by themselves or other refugees to create their own separate rebellion.


Duties:

  • Fight against the Combine in City 17 RP and City 24 RP.

  • Craft weapons and help any rebels to defeat the Combine in Outland RP.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has limited access to the rebel base during Outland RP.

  • Has ability to spawn the crafting table.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • Don't kill other refugees or rebels unless they are betraying the rebellion.

  • Don't kill civilians in City 17 RP or City 24 RP unless you know they are helping the combine fight you in some way.
