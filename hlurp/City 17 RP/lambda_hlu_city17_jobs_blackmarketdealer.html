In-Game Description:

The black market dealer is a rebel who has stayed in the city to help refugees fight the combine by spawning them ammo and armor. Black market dealers can choose to whether sell their items to refugees or just give it away for free. Please note that as this job you cannot kill any players, you are simply responsible for supplying the refugees.


Duties:

  • Help refugees by supplying them with ammo and armor.

  • Stay out of sight of the Combine or you may be captured or killed.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has ability to spawn small armor batteries.

  • Has ability to spawn various ammo boxes.

  • Has access to the disguise kit.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • You are not permitted to kill as this job unless it is self defense.

  • Using armor batteries for yourself is permitted, as long as you aren't in combat.

  • To combine jobs: you cannot kill a disguised black market dealer unless he/she has a rebel playermodel.
