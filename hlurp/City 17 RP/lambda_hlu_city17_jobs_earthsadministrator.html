In-Game Descripton:

Earth's Administrator is the former head of Black Mesa, and later named Earth's Admin under the Combine. From his headquarters on Earth in the Citadel of City 17, he is humanity's representative within the Combine.


Duties:

  • Keep City 17 in order and regularly have meetings with Combine forces.

  • Make city-wide alert announcements if necessary.

In the Event of an Emergency:

If something happens and action need to be taken, there are 3 things you can do as Earth's Administrator. You can either 1) Call a judgment waiver, 2) Call an autonomous waiver, or 3) Call a city lockdown.


Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has access to entire city.

  • Has access to enter the citadel/Combine bases.

  • Has access to enter the Earth Admin's office.

  • Has ability to control the Combine force.

  • Has access to Combine weapon shipments.

  • Has ability to convert a refugee into an Overwatch Soldier using the Combine Force SWEP.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • Do not leave the citadel without an escort.

  • Take your job seriously, this is one of the most important ones.

  • Only use your weapon shipments in certain areas of the Citadel.

  • You cannot use weapons for self defense if there are 4 or more combine units on the server.

  • You can only deploy soldiers, guards, and elites if the situation calls for any of them. For example, don't send out elites to patrol early in the game and rebels have even shown activity yet, and don't send out soldiers to raid an apartment for a small violation. Use metrocops to patrol and raid until the rebels have started to become an effective resistance.

  • Breaking these rules could result in a job ban.
