In-Game Descripton:

WARNING! ONLY BECOME THIS JOB IF THERE IS AN ACTIVE CITY ADMINISTRATOR! The City 17 Representative is a high-ranking Combine official who comes to City 24 on behalf of the Earth's Administrator to help out in controlling the city. If the City Administrator is somehow killed, the City 17 Representative takes charge until someone else can fill in for the City Admin.


Duties:

  • Visit City 24 and help the City Administrator keep the city in order.

In the Event of an Emergency:

If something happens and action need to be taken, there are 3 things you can do as the City Administrator. You can either 1) Call a judgment waiver, 2) Call an autonomous waiver, or 3) Call a city lockdown.


Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has access to entire city, unless the City Administrator says otherwise.

  • Has access to enter the citadel/Combine bases, unless the City Administrator says otherwise.

  • Has ability to take control of the city if something happens to the City Administrator.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • You are not permitted to kill as this job unless it is self defense. Breaking this rule could result in a job ban.
