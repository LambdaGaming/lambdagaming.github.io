In-Game Description:

Elites are tougher, achieve better accuracy with their weapons, and inflict more overall damage than regular soldiers do. They typically carry Overwatch Standard Issue Pulse Rifles, and are able to use the weapon's secondary fire Energy Ball. They use this advantage without hesitation and with deadly accuracy. Elites also may be picked to protect the Earth's Admin's office.


Duties:

  • Assist Overwatch Soldiers in firefights if needed.

  • Protect Earth's Administrator's office if he requests it.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has access to different Combine weaponry.

  • Has access to enter the citadel.

  • Has access to enter the Earth Admin's office.

  • Has ability to set up official Combine bases/hideouts for easy access to possible rebel activity.

  • Has access to Combine weapon shipments.

  • Has ability to convert a civilian into an Overwatch Soldier using the Combine Force SWEP.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • ONLY discharge your weapons if you see a threat. The only exception is target practice in the citadel prison.

  • Don't go to restricted parts of the citadel without permission from Earth's Administrator.

  • Patrolling is not your job. Only go into the streets if you are ordered to.

  • Only set up Combine bases/hideouts close to areas that you know have rebel activity.

  • Only use your weapon shipments at official Combine bases, nowhere else.

  • Don't abuse the Combine Force SWEP. Only use it on prisoners and law breakers that you don't want to execute.

  • Breaking these rules could result in a job ban.
