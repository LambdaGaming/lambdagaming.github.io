In-Game Description:

Civilians are people who survived the Seven Hour War, and were relocated to City 17 (or City 24) to be forced into a form of slavery. Every civilians top priority is to do what the Combine tells them to do. Civilians are for the most part loyal to the Combine and will do what they are told, so they are not permitted to craft weapons and fight the Combine.


Duties:

  • Listen to metrocops and Earth's Administrator (or City Administrator) for your daily duties. If you aren't told to do anything, ask a member of the Combine and they will give you orders.

  • Take your food supplies every day from the plaza food distributor.

  • Help the Combine search for potential rebels throughout the city.

  • Help the Combine by mining crafting materials for them.

  • Mine rocks and turn the ores into the Combine for a profit.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • None

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • You are not permitted to kill as this job unless it is self defense.

  • Don't enter places you know you aren't allowed to enter, or else you may be killed or arrested.
