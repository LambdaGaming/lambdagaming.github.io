City 17 RP Job: Vortigaunt

In-Game Description:

The vortigaunt is an alien species that has been enslaved by the Combine alongside humanity. Vorts are able to shoot fatal amounts of electricity from their hands and are very strong, allowing them to do things faster and easier compared to humans. By default, Vorts are under the rule of the Combine, but can be put under rebel command if given the opportunity.


Duties:

  • Follow orders from either the Combine or rebels.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has ability to shoot lightning as well as mine rocks faster than humans.

Job-Specific Rules:

  • You must follow all orders from whoever is commanding you. By default, it's going to be the Combine.

  • You can choose to be independent or try to work for both sides but only if you're relieved of your duties by whichever side is commanding you.
