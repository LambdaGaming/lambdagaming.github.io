Visitor

Description

The Visitor is not employed and requires a security escort to get into the Black Mesa Research Facility. This is recommended if you are new to the server and/or map.

Duties

  • Learn about the server. (If new to the gamemode.)

  • Take a tour of the map with the Administrator or Head of Security. (If new to the map.)

Tips

  • Don't stay as a visitor for a long time. Once you know the basics of the server, get started working at the facility!

Facility Administrator

Description

The Administrator is pretty much the opposite of the visitor. He has access to the entire facility, as well as control over everyone in it. He is also responsible for ensuring the safety of staff as well as making sure everyone is on-task and doing their job correctly.

Duties

  • Assign tasks for certain jobs through the F1 menu.

  • Ensure everyone is being productive and safe.

  • Give informational tours to visitors who want one.

  • Listen to the Government Man for suggestions and concerns.

Abilities & Items

  • Default Weapons - Handcuffs

  • F1 Menu - This menu gives you the ability to assign either pre-made or custom tasks to various jobs. You can also reward people for completing tasks by giving them funds.

Technician

Description

As a technician, your job is to work on all types of computer systems. You can also help other scientists if they have a question about Wiremod or GTerminal. Recommended for advanced Wiremod users.

Duties

  • Build servers using GTerminal and PCMod.

  • Build and set up computer systems for other players who need one.

  • Build advanced wiremod creations that could highly benefit the facility.

Abilities & Items

  • F3 Menu Items - Terminal I/O Device, Electric generator

  • PCMod Tools - The technician has exclusive access to all PCMod tools for building server networks and personal computers.

Biochemist

Description

Your main job is to study the crystals the Survey Team brings back from Xen. If you have the time, you can also study living things such as aliens and plant life.

Duties

  • Study crystals brought back from Xen by the Survey Team.

  • Study organic matter, such as aliens and plants from Xen.

Abilities & Items

  • F3 Menu Items - Biochemist crafting table, Chemical container, Nuclear reactor, Radioactive material barrel.

Medic

Description

The medic is responsible for ensuring everyone in the facility is healthy and safe by healing people and charging HEV suits.

Duties

  • Respond to medical emergencies to heal people.

  • Setup health and armor stations around the facility so you don't have to constantly run around.

Abilities & Items

  • Default Weapons - Med kit

  • F3 Menu Items - Armor battery, Health charger, Health kit, Suit charger.

Service Official

Description

As a Service Official, your job is to clean up the facility by cleaning up spills/stains, inspecting various machines, extinguishing fires, and throwing away unused materials.

Duties

  • Extinguish any fires that may break out.

  • Tidy up the facility by throwing away unused crafting materials.

  • Use the broom to clear unwanted decals such as blood or bullet holes.

  • Inspect anything that you are asked to.

Abilities & Items

  • Default Weapons - Broom, Crowbar, Fire extinguisher, Pipe wrench, Trash bag

  • F3 Menu Items - Fire extinguisher, Vending machine resupply.

Government Man

Description

The Government Man (or G-Man) walks around the facility looking at the conditions of certain things. Anything that looks unsafe should be reported to the facility administrator and/or the head of security. It would be quite unfortunate if an...accident...were to happen...hmm?

Duties

  • Monitor things around the facility to ensure everything is done by the book.

  • Express any concerns with the facility administrator and/or head of security and ensure things get fixed as needed

  • Talk with your employers about certain.....events...that could take place if staff were to ignore safety precautions. (F1 menu)

Abilities & Items

  • Default Weapons - Teleporter

  • F1 Menu - The F1 menu allows the government man to activate certain events that could either help or harm Black Mesa depending on how they respond to it.

Tips

  • It is highly recommended you pay attention to what is happening in the facility and call events based on that, instead of just picking a random one.

HECU Marine

Description

Note: Under normal conditions, players can only join the HECU after escaping the facility. As a HECU Marine, your job is to protect Black Mesa from major hazards that could mean life or death for Black Mesa personnel. You listen and take orders from the HECU Commander. Having this job is only recommended when security needs backup.

Duties

  • Protect Black Mesa from extreme hazards such as a major alien invasion, or a dangerous machinery malfunction.

  • Help the Black Mesa Security Force patrol sectors and keep all personnel safe.

Abilities & Items

  • Default Weapons - Battering ram, Pistol, Shotgun.

HECU Captain

Description

Note: Under normal conditions, players can only join the HECU after escaping the facility. As the HECU Captain, your job is to set up a team of marines to assist Black Mesa security in major hostile events. You can also command your marines in non-hostile events so they can patrol with security or help a fellow marine in training.

Duties

  • Command and control your marines so they can help security with patroling and emergency situations.

  • Help train new marines and security officers.

  • Detonate the facility with a nuclear weapon if absolutely necessary.

Abilities & Items

  • Default Weapons - Battering ram, Desert Eagle, M16.

  • Thermonuclear Device - Nuke that can be spawned from the HECU weapon crate found within the facility.

Weapon Specialist

Description

As a Weapon Specialist, you are in charge of all weapons at Black Mesa. You are to supply your fellow marines as well as the Black Mesa Security Force with weapons. You can also request a custom weapon to be made by the weapons engineer, such as a self-destructing ground unit, or a drone with a mounted weapon.

Duties

  • Supply weapons and ammo to Marines and Security.

  • Ask the Weapons Engineer to build custom weapons that will benefit your fellow Marines and Security Officers.

  • Join your fellow Marines to contain Black Mesa in the event of an emergency.

Abilities & Items

  • Default Weapons - Pistol, Shotgun

  • F3 Menu Items - Various military-grade weapon shipments

Weapons Engineer

Description

As a weapons engineer, your job is to make, test, and modify weapons for the H.E.C.U. to use. If you are not assigned to a specific weapon to work on, have a talk with the H.E.C.U. Weapon Specialist and they will tell you what to work on. A general skill with wiremod turrets is recommended for this job.

Duties

  • Experiment with different weaponry that could benefit the H.E.C.U. as well as Black Mesa Security.

  • Make specific weapons as requested by the Weapon Specialist.

Abilities & Items

  • F3 Menu Items - High-powered laser emitter, Weapons engineer crafting table.

  • Wiremod Tools - The weapons engineer has exclusive access to certain Wiremod tools including explosives and turrets.

Tips

  • Since you work alongside the military, you do not have to give away any information regarding your creations. However, if security needs to get into your lab, they have the right to enter.

Security Officer

Description

As a Security Officer, your job is to protect Black Mesa from anything that may put anyone's life at risk or anything that may get in the way of working. You are permitted to set up checkpoints if you are alone in a Sector and feel that you need it.

Duties

  • Keep the facility safe from any threats.

  • Set up checkpoints in high traffic areas so personnel have to show an ID before passing through.

Abilities & Items

  • Default Weapons - Battering ram, Handcuffs, Pistol

Head of Security

Description

As the Head of Security, your job is to command and take control of all security personnel and ensure the security of the entire facility.

Duties

  • Command and control all Security personnel.

  • Report security issues to the facility admin if you cannot fix them yourself.

Abilities & Items

  • Default Weapons - Battering ram, Handcuffs, Revolver, Shotgun

Survey Member

Description

As a Survey Team member, your job is to teleport to Xen and bring crystal samples and other materials back to Black Mesa for the Biochemist to research. Your job could be highly dangerous, so work with the medic and weapons engineer to ensure you are properly equipped for the task.

Duties

  • Participate in tests that require an HEV suit.

  • Collect objects from Xen and give them to Biochemists and Weapon Engineers so they can craft items.

Abilities & Items

  • Default Weapons - Pickaxe, Pistol, Stunstick

Head of Survey Team

Description

As the Head of the Survey Team, your job is to order and plan the trips to Xen. You are also required to go to Xen in the event that one of the other Survey Team members is in trouble. You have permission to use firearms, but only in the event of an alien encounter.

Duties

  • Order and plan trips to Xen for your team members.

  • Participate in tests that require an HEV suit.

  • Report to Xen if your team members require assistance.

  • Collect objects from Xen and give them to Biochemists and Weapon Engineers so they can craft items.

Abilities & Items

  • Default Weapons - M16, Pickaxe, Stunstick
