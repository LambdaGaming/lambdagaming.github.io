Visitor

In-Game Description:

The Visitor is not employed and requires a security escort to get into the Black Mesa Research Facility. This is recommended if you are new to the server.


Duties:

  • Learn about the server. (If new to the gamemode.)

  • Take a tour of the map with the Administrator, Head of Security, or HECU Captain. (If new to the map.)

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • None

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • Don't stay a visitor for a long time. Once you know the basics of the roleplay, get started working at the facility.

  • Don't avoid security to try and sneak your way into the facility.

Facility Administrator

Note: For a list of commands that go along with this job, click here.

In-game Description:

The Administrator is pretty much the opposite of the visitor. He has access to the entire facility, as well as control over everyone in it. As an administrator, you can escort visitors, command part of the military, call for fire drills, and, if needed, call for an emergency evacuation or lockdown. You talk with your on site-military and security to keep people safe, and you occasionally walk around the facility to check on things. Finally, and most importantly, it is your job to schedule Anomalous Materials tests on Xen specimens using the Anti-Mass Spectrometer (AMS). A test can only happen once every 2 hours real-time, so if you think you are not ready with the specimen, don't schedule a test.


Events:

The administrator can't be in charge of natural disasters like earthquakes and meteor storms, but he can ensure safety if something happens. To call a lockdown, simply type /lockdown into chat. If a lockdown is called, an alarm will sound server-wide, alerting all players. If an emergency evacuation is called, it is your job to go to the Sector C security room and activate the fire alarm. You can then make an advert explaining why people need to evacuate.


Duties:

  • Keep order throughout the facility.

  • Ensure safety of all personnel.

  • Give informational tours to visitors who want one.

  • Command all science, maintenance, and security personnel.

  • Listen to the Government Man and Safety Advisor for suggestions and concerns.

  • Have all personnel write reports of what they are doing, and have them delivered to you to look at.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has access to the lockdown command.

  • Has command over whole facility.

  • Has ability to arrest players.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • Don't abuse your power. Just because you are in charge of the facility doesn't mean you have to boss everyone around and ruin the game experience for others.

  • Don't spam lockdowns. Only call them if you feel that lives are in danger.

  • Don't tell the whole military what to do. It's ok if you are telling one or two marines to check something out, but bossing around the Captain or the whole military could result in bad things!

Research and Development

In-Game Description:

As a member of the Research and Development team, your job is to research and develop new technology for use in the B.M.R.F. Use of advanced Wiremod and Gterminal is highly recommended for this job. This job is similar to the Weapons Engineer, except for the fact this job uses simple Wiremod instead of Wiremod turrets and explosives.


Duties:

  • Make anything as long as it doesn't involve weapons or advanced wiremod technology.

  • When making something remember these things: 1) Does it not have flaws? 2) Does it function correctly? 3) Is it useful for anything? If all of these answers are "yes" for your creation, then you can show it off to the whole facility!

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Certain Wiremod tools that aren't available to any other job.

  • Ability to create and test pretty much anything you want.

Technician

In-Game Description:

As a technician, your job is to work on all types of computer systems. You can also help other scientists if they have a question about Wiremod or GTerminal. Recommended for advanced Wiremod users.


Duties:

  • Build servers using GTerminal and PCMod.

  • Build and set up computer systems for other players who need one.

  • Build advanced wiremod creations that could highly benefit the facility.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Ability to use certain Wiremod and PCMod tools that no other jobs have.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • Don't abuse your ability to use advanced wire creations. Don't annoy, kill, teleport, or trap people with it.

Biochemist

In-Game Description:

Your main job is to study the crystals the Survey Team brings back from Xen. If you have the time, you can study other biological and living things. You can study living things by using your NPC spawner tool. This tool is known throughout Black Mesa as a mobile-mini-dimensional-portal. It allows you to spawn any NPC in the game. Don't abuse it by spamming the NPC count though; you may be banned from this job for doing so.


Duties:

  • Study crystals brought back from Xen by the Survey Team. You can take notes on the color, shape, width, length, thickness, and radiation levels of each crystal.

  • Take crystals to AMS control room for testing after you are done researching them.

  • Study organic matter, such as aliens and plants from Xen.

Special Job-Specific Items/Tips:

  • Ability to spawn and craft various alien NPCs as well as work in the Sector C Bio Labs, which are restricted to most jobs

  • Has access to the biochemist crafting table.

Medic

In-Game Description:

With your medical knowledge you work to restore players to full health. Left click with the Medical Kit to heal other players. Right click with the Medical Kit to heal yourself.


Duties:

  • Respond to medical emergencies to heal people.

  • Set up an office in Sector D and let people know where your office is located in case of emergency.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has ability to heal any player as well as spawn health kits and health machines.

Service Official

In-Game Description:

As a Service Official, your job is to clean up the facility by painting over spills/stains, inspecting various machines, extinguishing fires, and cleaning up stray props. This is the only job that has unlimited access to maintenance areas around the facility. You are equipped with a fire extinguisher in case things get out of hand. If a fire does break out, contain it. If you see a fire that is spreading, call in for more service personnel help you contain the fire. You are also required to report any fires to the safety advisor or administrator.


Duties:

  • Extinguish any fires that may break out.

  • Tidy up the facility by putting crates back in order, putting wet floor signs down if needed, etc.

  • Use the paint tool to cover up stains that may appear on the map.

  • Inspect anything that you are asked to.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has access to a fire extinguisher, broom, and most areas of the facility.

  • Has ability to repair machines such as generators.

Government Man

In-Game Description:

The Government Man (or G-Man) walks around the facility looking at the conditions of certain things. You should first take a tour with the administrator or head of security, then later talk with the Safety Advisor about things that could be improved. Once you are done with that, you can continue to walk around the facility asking the administrator questions if needed. Once you are free, you can spend the rest of your time looking at certain parts of the facility by yourself.


In the Event of an Emergency:

If something such as a cascade happens, it is your responsibility to call in the military. To do this, simply send out an advert (in sort of a "roleplay" form) stating that the military is being called in to contain the situation. Once a server admin sees the advert, one admin must become an HECU Captain. Once there is an HECU Captain on the server, the admin can then announce that he is now recruiting anyone who wants to be a marine. Once there is a HECU squad formed, they can then proceed to stock up on weapons and ammo in the on-site base, and proceed to enter the facility. As government man, you also have the ability to choose what the marines do. They can either 1) Rescue all personnel and take them up for questioning, or 2) Kill all personnel and cover up the incident from the rest of the world. Keep in mind, though, that if the HECU Captain does not agree, he doesn't have to follow your orders, so you are pretty much only suggesting in this case.


Duties:

  • Take a tour of the facility with the administrator.

  • Talk with the Safety Advisor and administrator to see what could be improved.

  • Call in the military if needed.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has access to the whole facility as well as the on-site military base without anyone's permission.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • Don't abuse your ability to call in the military, only do it if there is a major security or safety hazard.

HECU Marine

In-Game Description:

WARNING! CASCADE EVENT ONLY! USING THIS JOB WITHOUT ADMIN APPROVAL WILL GET YOU DEMOTED BACK TO CIVILIAN! As a HECU Marine, your job is to protect Black Mesa from major hazards that could mean life or death for Black Mesa personnel. You listen and take orders from the HECU Commander. Having this job is only recommended when security needs backup. Keep in mind that this job can only be accessed by volunteering to become a marine once the HECU Captain has announced that he is recruiting.


Duties:

  • Protect Black Mesa from extreme hazards such as a major alien invasion, or a dangerous machinery malfunction.

  • Help the Black Mesa Security Force patrol sectors and keep all personnel safe.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has ability to command and control lower-ranking personnel such as scientists and security officers in emergency situations.

  • Has access to military-grade weapons and equipment as well as the on-site military base.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • Don't go to restricted parts of the facility without permission from the Head of Security or Administrator.

HECU Captain

Note: For a list of commands that go along with this job, click here.

In-Game Description

WARNING! CASCADE EVENT ONLY! USING THIS JOB WITHOUT ADMIN APPROVAL WILL GET YOU DEMOTED BACK TO CIVILIAN! As the HECU Captain, your job is to set up a team of marines to assist Black Mesa security in major hostile events. You can also command your marines in non-hostile events so they can patrol with security or help a fellow marine in training.


Duties:

  • Command and control your marines so they can help security with patroling and emergency situations.

  • Help train new marines and security officers.

  • Talk with the Administrator and Government Man about security concerns if needed.

In the Event of an Emergency:

If a major event happens, such as a cascade, you have to pick what you want to do with your squad. You can either (1)Send out a search and rescue order so your marines save all personnel and bring them topside for questioning or (2)Send out an order to kill all living organisms to try and cover up what happened from the rest of the world.


Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has ability to command all marines.

  • Has ability to command and control lower-ranking personnel such as scientists and security officers in emergency situations.

  • Has access to military-grade weapons as well as the on-site military base.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • You only officially have command over your squad of marines, so don't go around the facility thinking you own the place.

Weapon Specialist

In-Game Description:

As a Weapons Specialist, you are in charge of all weapons at Black Mesa. You are to supply your fellow marines as well as the Black Mesa Security Force with weapons. You can also request a custom weapon to be made by the weapons engineer, such as a self-destructing ground unit, or a drone with a mounted weapon.


Duties:

  • Supply weapons and ammo to Marines and Security.

  • Ask the Weapons Engineer to build custom weapons that will benefit your fellow Marines and Security Officers.

  • Join your fellow Marines to contain Black Mesa in the event of an emergency.

  • Help train future Marines and/or security officers using the on-site military training course.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has access to all official weapons used throughout Black Mesa.

  • Has ability to request custom weapons be built by the Weapons Engineer.

Weapons Engineer

In-Game Description:

As a weapons engineer, your job is to make, test, and modify weapons for the H.E.C.U. to use. If you are not assigned to a specific weapon to work on, have a talk with the H.E.C.U. Weapon Specialist and they will tell you what to work on. A general skill with wiremod turrets is recommended for this job.


Duties:

  • Experiment with different weaponry that could benefit the U.S. Military as well as Black Mesa Security.

  • Make specific weapons asked by the Weapon Specialist.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has access to certain wiremod turrets, explosives, and targeting systems.

  • Has ability to work directly with the HECU and security.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • Since you work alongside the military, you do not have to give away any information regarding your creations. However, if security needs to get into your lab, they have the right to enter.

  • Don't build machinery that you know will get stuck in doorways and such. If the Weapon Specialist asks you to build something like this, tell him/her that you can only make it as big as a standard door.

Security Officer

In-Game Description:

As a Security Officer, your job is to protect Black Mesa from anything that may put anyone's life at risk or anything that may get in the way of working. You are permitted to set up checkpoints if you are alone in a Sector and feel that you need it. You can also request HECU assistance if things really get out of hand. If you do call them in though, they have permission to command you, whether you like it or not.


Duties:

  • Keep the facility safe from any threats.

  • Patrol certain sectors of the facility.

  • Set up checkpoints in high traffic areas so personnel have to show an ID before passing through.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has ability to call in HECU support, setup checkpoints, and patrol anywhere unless assigned a specific sector.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • Ask permission from either the head of security or a server admin before setting up a checkpoint. This is only in place so players don't set up checkpoints in areas that shouldn't have one.

Head of Security

In-Game Description:

As the Head of Security, your job is to command and take control of all security personnel. You are required to report to the Sector C test lab if the administrator calls for a test. You are also one of the few people who are required to pull the fire alarm in the event of an emergency. The fire alarm is in the room located next to the main entrance of Sector C.


Duties:

  • Command and control all Security personnel.

  • Pull the fire alarm in the event of an emergency.

  • Give permission to HECU Marines to enter the facility if they ask. (You don't have to if you feel that they don't need to come in at that time.)

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has access to secure areas as well as Black Mesa standard sidearms and shotguns.

  • Has ability to command and control all Security personnel and other personnel if needed.

Job-Specific Rules:

  • Only command your officers when you need them to do something. Don't go around the facility commanding everyone for no reason.

Survey Member

In-Game Description:

As a Survey Team member, your Job is to use the Sector A teleportation unit to teleport to Xen and bring crystal samples and live aliens back to Black Mesa for the Biochemist to research. If you fail to bring back any live creatures, don't worry, the Biochemist may have the technology to manually bring in a live creature from Xen. Your job could be highly dangerous, so call the head of the Survey Team if you need assistance. Lastly, you are required to report to the AMS in Sector C when the administrator wants to perform a test if there is no Head of Survey Team to insert the specimen.


Duties:

  • Collect specimens from the border world dimension Xen.

  • Insert specimens into the AMS if no Head of Survey Team is on duty to do so.

  • Collect objects from Xen and give them to Biochemists and Weapon Engineers so they can craft items.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has access to a pistol and stunstick by default as well as Xen.

Head of Survey Team

In-Game Description:

As the Head of the Survey Team, your job is to order and plan the trips to Xen. You are also required to go to Xen in the event that one of the other Survey Team members is in trouble. You have permission to use firearms, but only in the event of an alien encounter. You will also be required to report to Sector C if the administrator wants to run a test as you will be the one to handle the specimen.


Duties:

  • Order and plan trips to Xen for your team members.

  • Report to the AMS to insert the specimen when the administrator tells you to.

  • Report to Xen if your team members require assistance.

  • Collect objects from Xen and give them to Biochemists and Weapon Engineers so they can craft items.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has access to a rifle and stunstick by default as well as Xen.

  • Has ability to command the Survey Team.
