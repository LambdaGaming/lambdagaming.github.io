Loyal Citizen

Description:

Loyal citizens are people who survived the Seven Hour War, and were relocated to City 17 to be forced into a form of slavery. Every loyal citizens top priority is to do what the Combine tells them to do. Local citizens are, as you might expect, loyal to the Combine and will do what they are told, so they are not permitted to craft weapons and fight the Combine.


Duties:

  • Listen to metrocops and Earth's Administrator for your daily duties. If you aren't told to do anything, ask a member of the Combine and they will give you orders.

  • Take your food supplies every day from the plaza food distributor.

  • Help the Combine search for potential rebels throughout the city.

  • Help the Combine by mining crafting materials for them.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • You are not permitted to kill as this job unless it is self defense.

  • (Tip) You can help improve your relationship with the Combine by doing work for them, reporting any suspicious behavior, and staying away from restricted areas.

Refugee

Description:

Refugees are civilians of City 17 who have decided to fight against the combine. They typically create bases in areas of the city that are inaccessible to normal citizens to avoid detection.


Duties:

  • Help build up an effective resistance against the Combine while remaining undetected.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • (Entity) Rebel crafting table

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • Don't kill other refugees or rebels unless they are betraying the rebellion.

  • Don't kill civilians unless you know they are helping the Combine fight you in some way.

Resistance Leader

Description:

The resistance leader is in charge of commanding the rebel force. It is their duty to recruit any refugees who are looking to fight the combine. It is recommended that you say hidden indoors and underground. Since you are one of the highest ranking members of the resistance, you cannot afford to get yourself captured and forced by the combine to give out resistance intel.


Duties:

  • Recruit refugees who are looking to rebel against the combine.

  • Stay out of sight of the Combine or you may be captured or killed.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • (Weapon) SMG

  • (Weapon) Disguise kit

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • (Tip) It's highly recommended that you stay out of sight to avoid being captured. If you do have to go outside though, make sure you are secured by plenty of refugees or at least have a disguise on.

Scientist

Description:

As a scientist, you can choose to work for either the Combine or rebels (or both if you can manage to pull it off). Your task is to help either side develop special items or abilities.


Duties:

  • Help the Combine research and develop advanced weapons and units through the science locker.

  • Help the rebels develop a decryption device to be used on the detonation codes at the top of the citadel.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Access to the Combine science locker and Combine crafting table.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • You are free to choose to work with whoever you want, but refusing to work with both sides may get you banned from this job.

Vortigaunt

Description:

The vortigaunt is an alien species that has been enslaved by the Combine alongside humanity. Vorts are able to shoot fatal amounts of electricity from their hands and are very strong, allowing them to do things faster and easier compared to humans. By default, Vorts are under the rule of the Combine, but can be put under rebel command if given the opportunity.


Duties:

  • Follow orders from either the Combine or rebels.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • (Weapon) Vortigaunt beam

  • Ability to mine faster than humans.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • You must follow all orders from whoever is commanding you. By default, it's going to be the Combine.

  • You can choose to be independent or try to work for both sides but only if you're relieved of your duties by whichever side is commanding you.

Government Man

Description:

The Government Man for City 17 RP works like a spy. He can choose a side (combine or rebels) and help them by giving them advantages such as hidden base locations and attack plans. The Government Man can take two sides at once if he pleases. Players can turn on the Government Man if they have reason to believe he is a spy for the enemy, but they must have solid evidence before killing.


Duties:

  • Give either the Combine, Rebels, or both teams advantages by giving away secrets and base locations.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • (Weapon) Teleporter that allows for returning back to a saved position.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • (Tip) If you decide to help out both sides, try not to give away too much info about each side; it might result in you being killed for spying.

Cremator

Description:

The cremator, much like the HECU in BMRP, are to only be used in the most extreme circumstances that Combine forces cannot handle alone. Cremators are equipped with flamethrowers, a very effective tool especially against the undead.


Duties:

  • Assist other units where needed.

  • Contain any and all outbreaks.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • (Weapon) Flamethrower

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • (Tip) This is a special job that needs to be researched by a scientist in order to unlock.

Metrocop

Description:

Civil protection units are unmodified humans who have decided to join the ranks of the Combine. They mainly patrol apartment blocks and low-security checkpoints, and they take orders from all other Combine units.


Duties:

  • Keep the city safe from any threats.

  • Follow orders from all other Combine roles.

  • Set up checkpoints in high traffic areas so citizens have to show an ID before passing through.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • (Weapon) Pistol

  • (Weapon) Stunstick

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • You can prop block for checkpoints, but you still need to let players through if you know they live in that certain sector.

Overwatch Soldier

Description:

Overwatch soldiers are the lowest level of trans-human units. Unlike civil protection, overwatch soldiers take on a more militaristic role and see much more action, but also longer periods of inactivity. When raiding rebel bases, these units are typically on the front lines.


Duties:

  • Follow orders from overwatch elites and earth's administrator.

  • Help raid known rebel bases and assist civil protection in firefights.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • (Weapon) SMG

  • (Weapon) Grenade

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • Your duty is not to patrol, so do not go patrolling with civil protection.

Overwatch Guard

Description:

Overwatch Guards are in charge of defending and restricting access to the Citadel and any other places that may need to have a constant Combine presence.


Duties:

  • Follow orders from overwatch elites and earth's administrator.

  • Protect all restricted areas that are under Combine control, but mainly the citadel.

  • Act as a field medic if necessary.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • (Weapon) Sniper rifle

  • (Weapon) Binoculars

  • (Weapon) Medkit

Overwatch Elite

Description:

The Overwatch elite, as the name implies, is the most elite unit of the trans-human army. Their humanity has been completely stripped away and what remains is a ruthless killing machine. These units are designed to be frontline soldiers in the worst environments imaginable.


Duties:

  • Assist Overwatch Soldiers in firefights if needed.

  • Protect the Earth Admin's office if he requests it.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • (Weapon) AR2

  • (Entity) Various low-quantity weapon shipments

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • You are permitted to take the role of the Earth Admin if there is currently no Earth Admin present and there are 5 or less players on the server.

  • Patrolling is not your job. Only go into the streets if you are ordered to.

  • (Tip) Only setup your weapon shipments in highly secured areas to prevent them from falling into rebel hands.

Earth's Administrator

Descripton:

Earth's Administrator is the former head of Black Mesa, and later named Earth's Admin under the Combine. He lives at the top of the citadel and commands all human and trans-human units of the Combine.


Duties:

  • Keep City 17 in order and command all Combine units.

  • Make city-wide announcements to gain loyalty and inform citizens of important things.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • (Weapon) Announcement tool

  • (Entity) Various high-quantity weapon shipments.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • You cannot use weapons for self defense if there are 4 or more combine units on the server.

  • You can only deploy soldiers, guards, and elites if the situation calls for any of them. For example, don't send out elites to patrol early in the game and rebels have even shown activity yet, and don't send out soldiers to raid an apartment for a small violation. Use metrocops to patrol and raid until the rebels have started to become an effective resistance.

  • (Tip) Do not leave the citadel without an escort to avoid being captured or killed.

  • (Tip) Only setup your weapon shipments in highly secured areas to prevent them from falling into rebel hands.
