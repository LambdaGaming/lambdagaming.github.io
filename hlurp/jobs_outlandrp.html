Resistance Leader

Description

The resistance leader is in charge of commanding the rebel force. The resistance leader is also in charge of the rocket at the rebel base and when it launches. It is recommended that you say hidden indoors and underground. Since you are one of the highest ranking members of the resistance, you cannot afford to get yourself captured and forced by the combine to give out resistance intel.

Duties

  • Recruit refugees who are looking to rebel against the combine.

  • Launch the rocket and close the Combine portal when the time is right, OR raid the Combine's base and forcefully destroy the portal.

Abilities & Items

  • Default Weapons - Disguise kit, Portal pairing tool, SMG

  • F3 Menu Items - Rebel crafting table, ground scanner, sentry, thermoelectric generator, smelting furnace, auger drill, workbench.

Tips

  • It's highly recommended that you stay out of sight to avoid being captured. If you do have to go outside though, make sure you are secured by plenty of refugees or at least have a disguise on.

Rebel

Description

Rebels are past civilians of City 17 who have joined the rebellion in order to stop the Combine and make the world free once again. Rebels are usually equipped with SMGs, however it is very possible to pick up better weapons dropped by killed Combine Soldiers.

Duties

  • Protect the rebel base from Combine invasions.

  • Help out or work with any refugees you may come into contact with.

Abilities & Items

  • Default Weapons - Pickaxe, SMG

  • F3 Menu Items - Rebel crafting table, ground scanner, sentry, thermoelectric generator, smelting furnace, auger drill, workbench.

Rebel Medic

Description

Rebels are past civilians of City 17 who have joined the rebellion in order to stop the Combine and make the world free once again. Rebels are usually equipped with SMGs, however it is very possible to pick up better weapons dropped by killed Combine Soldiers.

Duties

  • Protect the rebel base from Combine invasions.

  • Help out or work with any refugees you may come into contact with.

Abilities & Items

  • Default Weapons - Med kit, Pickaxe, Pistol

Refugee

Description

Refugees are civilians of City 17 who have decided to fight against the combine. They have the option to act as guards for rebel bases, but they can also choose to work by themselves or other refugees to create their own separate rebellion.

Duties

  • Craft weapons and help any rebels to defeat the Combine in Outland RP, or survive on your own.

Abilities & Items

  • Default Weapons - Pickaxe

  • F3 Menu Items - Rebel crafting table, ground scanner, sentry, thermoelectric generator, smelting furnace, auger drill, workbench.

Overwatch Elite

Description

The Overwatch elite, as the name implies, is the most elite unit of the trans-human army. Their humanity has been completely stripped away and what remains is a ruthless killing machine. These units are designed to be frontline soldiers in the worst environments imaginable.

Duties:

  • Command what remains of the Combine.

  • Protect the Combine base from being overrun by rebels.

  • Assist your teammates in firefights if needed.

  • Stop the rebels from launching their rocket.

Abilities & Items

  • Default Weapons - AR2, Combine authorization tool, Frag grenade, Handcuffs.

  • Combine Vehicle Shop - Combine jobs have access to a vehicle shop at their base that allows them to spawn things such as manhacks, hunter choppers, and striders.

Overwatch Guard

Description

Overwatch Guards are in charge of defending and restricting access to the Combine base and any other places that may need to have a constant Combine presence.

Duties

  • Follow orders from overwatch elites.

  • Protect all restricted areas that are under Combine control, but primarily the main base.

  • Act as a field medic if necessary.

Abilities & Items

  • Default Weapons - Binoculars, Combine authorization tool, Handcuffs, Med kit, Pistol, Sniper rifle.

  • Combine Vehicle Shop - Combine jobs have access to a vehicle shop at their base that allows them to spawn things such as manhacks, hunter choppers, and striders.

  • F3 Menu Items - Armor battery, Health charger, Health kit, Suit charger.

Overwatch Soldier

Description

Overwatch soldiers are the lowest level of trans-human units. Unlike civil protection, overwatch soldiers take on a more militaristic role and see much more action, but also longer periods of inactivity. When raiding rebel bases, these units are typically on the front lines.

Duties

  • Follow orders from overwatch elites.

  • Help raid known rebel bases.

Abilities & Items

  • Default Weapons - Combine authorization tool, Flare gun, Frag grenade, Handcuffs, SMG.

  • Combine Vehicle Shop - Combine jobs have access to a vehicle shop at their base that allows them to spawn things such as manhacks, hunter choppers, and striders.
