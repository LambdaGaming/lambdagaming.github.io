Resistance Leader

In-Game Description:

The resistance leader is in charge of commanding the rebel force. The resistance leader is also in charge of the rocket at the rebel base and when it launches. It is recommended that you say hidden indoors and underground. Since you are one of the highest ranking members of the resistance, you cannot afford to get yourself captured and forced by the combine to give out resistance intel.


Duties:

  • Recruit refugees who are looking to rebel against the combine.

  • Stay out of sight of the Combine or you may be captured or killed.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has access to a SMG for defending refugees as well as a disguise kit.

  • Has ability to command refugees, spawn small armor batteries, and spawn various ammo boxes.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • It's highly recommended that you stay out of sight of the combine to avoid being captured. If you do have to go outside though, make sure you are secured by plenty of refugees or at least have a disguise on.

  • To combine jobs: you cannot kill a disguised resistance leader dealer unless he/she has a rebel or leader playermodel. (Examples of leader models: Eli, Alyx, Mossman, Barney, Odessa)

Rebel

In-Game Description:

Rebels are past civilians of City 17 who have joined the rebellion in order to stop the Combine and make the world free once again. Rebels are usually equipped with SMGs, however it is very possible to pick up better weapons dropped by killed Combine Soldiers.


Duties:

  • Protect the rebel base from Combine invasions.

  • Help out or work with any refugees you may come into contact with.

  • Attempt to destroy the combine portal by launching a rocket or breaking into the Combine base.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has access to the rebel base.

Rebel Medic

In-Game Description:

Rebels are past civilians of City 17 who have joined the rebellion in order to stop the Combine and make the world free once again. Rebels are usually equipped with SMGs, however it is very possible to pick up better weapons dropped by killed Combine Soldiers.


Duties:

  • Protect the rebel base from Combine invasions.

  • Help out or work with any refugees you may come into contact with.

  • Heal any injured rebels or refugees.

  • Attempt to destroy the combine portal by launching a rocket or breaking into the Combine base.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has access to the rebel base.

  • Has ability to heal other players.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • You cannot deny service to any rebels or refugees. Breaking this rule could result in a job ban.

Refugee

In-Game Description:

Refugees are civilians of City 17 who have decided to fight against the combine. They have the option to act as guards for rebel bases, but they can also choose to work by themselves or other refugees to create their own separate rebellion.


Duties:

  • Fight against the Combine in City 17 RP.

  • Craft weapons and help any rebels to defeat the Combine in Outland RP.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has limited access to the rebel base during Outland RP.

  • Has ability to spawn the crafting table.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • Don't kill other refugees or rebels unless they are betraying the rebellion.

  • Don't kill civilians in City 17 RP unless you know they are helping the combine fight you in some way.

Overwatch Elite

In-Game Description:

Elites are tougher, achieve better accuracy with their weapons, and inflict more overall damage than regular soldiers do. They typically carry Overwatch Standard Issue Pulse Rifles, and are able to use the weapon's secondary fire Energy Ball. They use this advantage without hesitation and with deadly accuracy. Elites also may be picked to protect the Earth's Admin's office.


Duties:

  • Assist Overwatch Soldiers in firefights if needed.

  • Protect Earth's Administrator's office if he requests it.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has access to weapon shipments and restricted areas of the citadel.

  • Has ability to set up official Combine bases/hideouts for easy access to possible rebel activity.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • ONLY discharge your weapons if you see a threat. The only exception is target practice in the citadel prison.

  • Don't go to restricted parts of the citadel without permission from Earth's Administrator.

  • Patrolling is not your job. Only go into the streets if you are ordered to.

  • Only set up Combine bases/hideouts close to areas that you know have rebel activity.

  • Only use your weapon shipments at official Combine bases, nowhere else.

Overwatch Guard

In-Game Description:

The Overwatch Guard is in charge of guarding and maintaining the Citadel and any other places that may have a major Overwatch presence. Guards can also use Combine sniper rifles and stand guard on top of a building.


Duties:

  • Protect restricted areas mainly in the citadel.

  • Report any suspicious activity that goes on to Earth's Administrator.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has access to the Combine sniper rifle and certain areas of the citadel.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • Don't go to restricted parts of the citadel without permission from Earth's Administrator.

Overwatch Soldier

In-Game Description:

Overwatch Soldiers are the basic trans-human infantry units of the Combine Overwatch, composing the backbone of the Combine's military presence on Earth. In contrast to Civil Protection, whose duties are to enforce order within suppressed population centers, soldiers of the Overwatch are tasked with more hazardous actions requiring skill and tact: from patrolling the treacherous borders of Combine-controlled territory to raiding Resistance-held strongholds.


Duties:

  • Guard the citadel walls and any other possible entrances to the citadel.

  • Help raid known rebel bases.

  • Assist metrocops in firefights.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has ability to be in the front line with metrocops when fighting rebels.

  • Has access to the Combine SMG and grenades as well as access to certain parts of the citadel.

Job-Specific Rules/Jobs:

  • Don't go to restricted parts of the citadel without permission from Earth's Administrator.

  • Your duty is not to patrol, so do not go patrolling with metrocops.
