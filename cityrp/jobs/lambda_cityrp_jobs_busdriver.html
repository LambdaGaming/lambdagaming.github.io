CityRP Job: Bus Driver

In-Game Description:

The bus driver's duty is just as the name suggests: to drive buses. To spawn your bus, go to the car dealer where you would spawn a normal civilian car. You may create a set of rules that your passengers must follow such as setting bus stop locations or having your passengers remain quiet while on the bus. If a passenger doesn't follow these rules, you have a right to deny them service and kick them off of your bus.


Duties:

  • Drive your bus around and make a profit off of transporting people.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has ability to spawn a bus at the civilian car dealer.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • You may only charge up to $200 for each passenger to ride your bus.

  • You cannot deny service to anyone unless they have done something to negatively affect you or they have broken one of your bus rules.
