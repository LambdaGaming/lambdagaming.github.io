CityRP Job: Tow Truck Driver

In-Game Description:

Tow truck drivers have the ability to tow vehicles if they are broken down, crashed, or illegally parked.


Duties:

  • Respond to players who call about a crashed or broken down vehicle.

  • Patrol the streets looking for illegally parked cars to tow away.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has ability to repair cars with a tool.

  • Has ability to tow cars.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • You can only charge up to $600 per tow/repair.

  • It is recommended that you adjust your charges according to distance towed and what percentage of vehicle health you had to restore.

  • You are not required to always tow a broken vehicle before fixing it, it is up to you whether to fix on site, tow before fixing, or to tow only if the vehicle is at a certain percentage.

  • You are not required to tow/repair all vehicles that need it, but it is recommended that you respond to all calls to maximize profits.
