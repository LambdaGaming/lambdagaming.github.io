CityRP Job: Police Chief

In-Game Description:

As Police Chief, it is your responsibility to command, control, and take care of your Officers.


Duties:

  • Command Police Officers, SWAT Commander, Undercover Officers.

  • Assist your officers with jobs.

  • Protect the mayor if no bodyguards are available.

  • Enforce all set laws.

  • Help out players throughout the city if needed.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has ability to command some units of the police force.

  • Has access to grenades, assault rifles, shotguns, and sidearms if the primary and secondary weapon perks are activated by the mayor.

  • Has access to a Desert Eagle sidearm by default.

  • Has access to the MP5 by default.

  • Has ability to weapon check players.

  • Has access to speed radar.

  • Has access to spike strips.

  • Has ability to spawn marked police vehicles.

  • Has ability to handcuff and arrest players.

  • Has access to the battering ram for breaking down doors.

Job-Specific Rules:

  • You may only tell the SWAT Commander what to do, you cannot individually command SWAT units unless there is no active SWAT Commander to do so.

  • You on the other hand get your orders from the mayor, who will usually tell you to enforce the new laws he has put down. Much like the bodyguards, you cannot deny orders from the mayor, but if you think an order is unlawful, talk it over with him.
