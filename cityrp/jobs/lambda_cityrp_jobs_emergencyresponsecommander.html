CityRP Job: Emergency Response Commander

In-Game Description:

As the emergency responder commander, your job is to command your team of emergency responders. You dont typically go and respond to emergencies, unless it is a major one and your team needs you. You spend most of your time inspecting unsafe fire hazards and talking with the mayor.


Duties:

  • Command emergency responders.

  • Fight fires and respond to medical emergencies yourself if needed.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has ability to command emergency responders.

  • Has ability to spawn fire trucks.

  • Has access to the fire extinguisher.

  • Has ability to heal players.

  • Has ability to spawn an ambulance.

  • Has access to narcan for drug-related emergencies.
