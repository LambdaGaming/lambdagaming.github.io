CityRP Job: Police Officer

In-Game Description:

The Police Officer may be the lowest ranking job with law enforcement, but it is undoubtedly the most important. As a Police Officer, your job is to patrol the streets, respond to emergencies, and look out for any suspicious activity.


Duties:

  • Enforce all set laws.

  • Help out players throughout the city if needed.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has ability to weapon check players, spawn government vehicles, and handcuff and arrest players.

  • Has access to grenades, assault rifles, shotguns, and sidearms if the primary and secondary weapon perks are activated by the mayor.

  • Has access to a Desert Eagle, MP5, speed radar, spike strips, and battering ram by default.

Job-Specific Rules:

  • A Police Officer's main duty is to follow orders from the police chief. If there is no police chief available, command will either go to SWAT or the mayor, depending on which is available first. If neither of those jobs are available to command, each individual officer will have to take all matters into their own hands.
