CityRP Job: Hitman

In-Game Description:

As a hitman, you earn money by taking hits from other players. To request a hit to a hitman, type !placehit in chat. You can then enter the player you want dead and the price you are paying to the hitman. If the hitman has a hit that he is currently on the job for, other hits cannot be placed. You can also earn extra money as a hitman by acting as a bounty hunter for government officials and getting paid to capture wanted players. Please note that as a hitman, you must supply your own weapons.


Duties:

  • Complete hits that other players order.

  • (Optional) Take bounties for government officials and capture wanted criminals.

Special Job-Specific Items:

  • Has ability to take hits via the hit menu.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • Be aware that you can be held responsible for killing players depending on the current laws.

  • If you notice that a hit is constantly being placed on the same person for no reason, ignore the hit requests.
