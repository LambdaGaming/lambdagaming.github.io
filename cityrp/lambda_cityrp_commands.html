The following is a complete list of chat commands and their functions for the CityRP server.



!fixeco Mayor only. Adds 1000 dollars to the mayor funds at the cost of 30 economy points.

!fireoff Superadmin only. Extinguishes all of the fires currently burning on the map.

!addons Available to all players. Opens the page to the addon collection in your Steam overlay.

!group Available to all players. Opens the page to the Lambda Gaming Steam group in your Steam overlay.

!alert Available to all players. Alerts all police and emergency responders to your location. Requires that you have life alert, which can be purchased at the NPC shop.

!firemayor IA Agent only. Fires the mayor for corruption and bans him from that job for 10 minutes.

!currentevent Available to all players. Prints the current event in chat. Prints N/A if there is currently no event active.

!disableproppay Superadmin only. Disables players having to pay to spawn props.

!enableproppay Superadmin only. Enables players having to pay to spawn props.

!noclip Admin only. Allows players to fly through solid objects.
