In-Game Description

As an emergency responder, your job is to respond to emergency calls concerning fires and medical emergencies. You are permitted to use either the fire truck or the ambulance for your duties.


Duties:

  • Extinguish fires using your equipment.

  • Respond to medical emergencies.

  • Take orders from your commander.

Special Job-Specific Items

  • Has ability to spawn fire trucks.

  • Has access to the fire extinguisher.

  • Has ability to heal players.

  • Has ability to spawn an ambulance.

  • Has access to narcan for drug-related emergencies.
