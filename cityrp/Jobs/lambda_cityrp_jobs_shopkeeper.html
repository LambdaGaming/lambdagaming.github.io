In-Game Description:

The shopkeeper is pretty much the same as the citizen job, except for the fact that the shopkeeper has the ability to spawn props and sell them to other players.


Duties:

  • Set up a store and sell props to players.

Special Job-Specific Items

  • Has ability to spawn props.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • You may charge any amount for the props you sell, but keep in mind that players are less likely to by very expensive props.

  • Note that props you buy will never exceed $100 in price.

  • You cannot deny service to anyone unless they have done something to negatively affect you.
