In-Game Description

As a SWAT Commander, you are responsible for commanding other SWAT units. You are required to take orders from the mayor and police chief. You are not required to take orders from federal agents. Aside from rank, the Commander differs from the regular SWAT units by not having grenades, having a shield, and having extra armor.


Duties:

  • Command other SWAT officers.

  • Respond to highly dangerous situations that officers can't deal with alone, such as drug busts or bank robberies.

Special Job-Specific Items

  • Has access to grenades, assault rifles, shotguns, and sidearms if the primary and secondary weapon perks are activated by the mayor.

  • Has access to an AR-15 by default.

  • Has access to a Desert Eagle sidearm by default.

  • Has access to the SWAT shield by default.

  • Has ability to weapon check players.

  • Has ability to spawn SWAT trucks.

  • Has access to the battering ram for breaking down doors.

  • Has access to the police leash.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • You cannot pull over or fine people as SWAT. If you see a law breaker, report it to officers. If there are no officers available, only then are you permitted to take actions into your own hands.
