In-Game Description

As an internal affairs agent, it is your responsibility to ensure that all government and law enforcement officials are following the laws they enforce, and are not being corrupt. If you do find corruption within the government, your duty is to fire or arrest the law breakers. Specifically, corruption not only includes government officials breaking their own laws, but it also includes the mayor killing or arresting multiple people for no apparent reason. You are equipped with a sidearm for protection just incase things go south.


Duties:

  • Investigate government and law enforcement officials for corruption if you suspect it.

  • Protect the mayor is there aren't any bodyguards or police officers to do so.

Special Job-Specific Items

  • Has access to a Glock pistol by default.

  • Has access to unmarked government vehicles.

  • Has ability to weapon check players.

  • Has ability to handcuff and arrest government and law enforcement officials.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • Don't arrest civilians for breaking the law, your job is to investigate law breakers within the city government.

  • Only arrest officials when you have solid evidence that they are corrupt.
