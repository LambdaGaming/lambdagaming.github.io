In-Game Description:

The Utility Worker is responsible for making sure the city is kept clean and in working order. Some of the duties the Utility Worker may have is to fine people for littering, enforce any building codes that the mayor may create, and fine people for illegal parking. If allowed to do so by the mayor, the utility worker can also run his own construction business.


Duties:

  • Fine players for littering. (Ex: Leaving props behind at an old base or leaving items next to the dumpster after dumpster diving.)

  • Fine players for parking illegally. (Only if there isn't a tow truck driver on the server.)

Special Job-Specific Items

  • Has access to free utility truck at the civilian car dealer.

  • Has ability to fine players using the fine menu.

Job-Specific Rules/Tips:

  • You can only use the fine menu to fine players for littering or illegal parking, nothing else.

  • Don't fine players for illegal parking if there is an active tow truck driver on the server to take care of it.
