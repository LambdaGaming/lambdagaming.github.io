Public Items:

ATM: The ATM is a machine used to deposit and withdraw money, as well as taking out loans. They are usually found inside city halls and banks.

Soda Machine: The soda machine is used to restore health. To use one, go up to it and press your use key, a soda will come out and you will automatically be deducted 30 dollars. To gain health, press your use key on the soda once it falls out of the machine. This item adds money to the government bank when used.

Item Bank: The item bank is a storage entity located in the bank of each map. It has the ability to store items from players' inventories. To use, go up to it and press your use key, you can then drag items from your inventory to the bank.

Police Bank Vault: The police bank vault is a small vault that can hold up to $10,000. To rob, press your use key on the money pile and wait for the timer to finish. Please note that only certain civilian-class jobs can rob this bank.

Government Bank Vault: The government bank vault is a large vault that can hold up to $50,000. To rob, press your use key on the money pallet and money bags will spawn in front of you. You can keep robbing until the pallet amount reaches zero. After you are done robbing, you must take the bags to the banker NPC to get the cash. Please note that only certain civilian-class jobs can rob this bank. This item subtracts money from the government bank when used.

NPC Shop: The NPC shop is used to buy items. The NPC shop has all legal items for sale, although some items that are sold can be used illegally. To use, press your use key on the NPC and select an item to buy.

Contraband NPC: The contraband NPC is used to buy illegal items. Most items sold by this NPC can get you fined or arrested if you are seen with them or using them, although the mayor can decide for each item whether it should be illegal or not.

Farm NPC: The farm NPC is used to sell freshly grown farm food. Be aware that you must place your food in a shipping crate, place the crate near the NPC, and press your use key on the crate for the NPC to accept the items.

Vehicle NPC: The vehicle NPC is used to buy, sell, and store vehicles. Be aware that when selling a vehicle, you will receive 75% of what you bought the vehicle for.

Mayor Election NPC: The mayor election NPC is used to enter the mayor elections. Once at least two players have entered the election, it will begin. Please note that elections will not start if there is already a mayor in office.

Rock and Tree Grinders: The rock and tree grinders are grinders/cutters that accept cartloads of logs or ores. To use, fill a rock or wood cart with the appropriate material, and take it to the appropriate grinder. Grinding time and cash earned depends on the density of the ores or logs.

Health NPC: The health NPC is used to restore heath and/or armor. Full health restoration costs $100, and full armor restoration costs $800. This item adds money to the government bank when used.

Fishing NPC: The fishing NPC is used to purchase fishing equipment such as bait, rods, and cages. Once you have caught some fish, you can go back to the NPC to sell them for a profit.

Dumpster: The dumpster used to "find" potentially valuable items. Valuable items have a 40% chance of spawning, and can range from simple fish bait to a pistol. Once a dumpster is used, a 2 minute cooldown will be in effect before someone else can use it.

Drug NPC: The drug NPC is used to sell market-grade drugs. To sell weed or meth, press your use key on a drug that is in range of the NPC. To sell cocaine, press your use key on the NPC with the cocaine in range of the NPC.

Banker NPC: The banker NPC is used to cash-in checks and money bags. To use, press your use key on a check that is in range of the NPC.

Smuggle Buy NPC: The smuggle buy NPC is used by criminals to buy illegal items to be shipped to a smuggle sell NPC. To use, press your use key and select an item to smuggle.

Smuggle Sell NPC: The smuggle sell NPC is used by criminals to sell illegal items that were bought from the smuggle buy NPC. To use, press your use key on the NPC, you will then receive payment for the smuggle and your shipping truck will be removed. This item subtracts money from the government bank when used.

Check Machine: The check machine is used mainly by the banker to spawn in checks to be cashed-in by the banker NPC. To use, press your use key on the machine and a check will be spawned on the floor. This item subtracts money from the government bank when used.

Courier NPC: The courier NPC is used to deliver packages to other players. By default, being a courier through this NPC is legal, but note that players can deny the package, and are allowed to call the police if you try to force them to take the package. You will receive a small reward every time you deliver a package.

Truck NPC and Trailer Kiosk: The truck NPC is used to rent trucks for transporting items. To use, press your use key on the NPC and you will be met with a menu to buy a truck. Once you press purchase, a truck will spawn outside and money will automatically be taken from your wallet. The trailer kiosk uses the same buying and spawning process as the truck.

Release NPC: The release NPC is used by criminals who have been arrested to break out of jail. To use, someone has to first break you out of your cell. You can then go to the police station front desk and press your use key on the NPC. A timer will then start for your release. Note that using this NPC alerts the police.

Purifier: The purifier is used to purify consumable weed into marketable weed. To use, touch consumable weed with the purifier. The item will be taken into the purifier for 45 seconds, then deposited next to the purifier. A basket will spawn next to the purifier for your convenience. The purifier has a limit of purifying 6 weed at a time.


Law Enforcement Items:

Speed Radar: The speed radar is a SWEP only available for police officers and is used to track the speed of a vehicle. To place it, aim close to the ground where you want it placed and left click. To remove it, walk up to it and right click. This item also has a feature that automatically fines players $50 if they are going over the speed limit.

Handheld Speed Radar: The handheld speed radar is a concealed version of the speed radar that only undercover officers have access to for more concealed traffic enforcement. Left click while looking at a vehicle to get a speed reading.

Police Handcuffs: The police handcuffs are used for restraining a player without arresting them. To cuff a player, left click. To uncuff a player, press your use key on the target player. This item is only available for the police chief, police officer, undercover officer, and detective.

Police Leash: The police leash is used by SWAT units to restrain a player without arresting them. The leash feature allows units to drag players who might otherwise run away with normal handcuffs. The leash has slightly less strength than the police handcuffs. To enable/disable the leash, press your reload key while looking at the player.

Police Badge: The police badge is used by all law enforcement officials to make sure they are official, and not fake officers.

Spikestrip: The spikestrip is used by all law enforcement officials to stop a vehicle. Once the vehicle has run over the strip, it's tires will pop and it will stop moving. If a spikestrip is left on the ground for more than 10 minutes, it will be auto removed and given back to the officer who placed it.

Taser: The taser is used by all law enforcement to stun someone and temporarily stop them from moving. Left click to fire, please note that you must be fairly close to the suspect for the taser to hit. A single use version of the taser is available at the contraband NPC for non-law enforcement players.

Handheld Metal Detector: The handheld metal detector is used by all law enforcement to quickly check for weapons without having the need to set up a normal metal detector. Left click to use, right click to toggle power. Please note that this item won't detect explosives.

Battering Ram: The battering ram is a tool used by law enforcement to break through doors after receiving a search warrant. To use, right click to hold it up into position, left click in front of a door to force it open.

Arrest/Unarrest Stick: The arrest and unarrest sticks are self explanatory, left click to arrest/unarrest a handcuffed player, right click to change type of stick.

Off-Duty Locker: The off-duty locker allows law enforcement officials to go off-duty and do their own personal RP things while still holding their job slot. When using this locker while on duty, all of your government-issued tools and weapons will be removed and your job name will automatically set to Off-Duty. When using this locker while off duty, your government-issued tools and weapons will be given back to you, and your job name will automatically change back to its default state.


Tow Truck Driver Items:

Tow SWEP: The tow cable SWEP is used for attaching a tow truck to another car. It is only available for the tow truck driver job. To use, back your tow truck up to the very front of the target car, and left click on the back of the tow truck with the cable SWEP equipped.

Tire Repair Tool: The tire repair tool is used for repairing popped tires. To use, go up to a vehicle and left click with the tool equipped. This item is available for purchase at the supermarket.

VCMod Repair Tool: The vehicle repair tool is used by tow truck drivers to fix up broken vehicles. To use, walk up to a vehicle and hold left click.

Car Bomb Protection: Used to prevent players from planting bombs on vehicles that have this equipped. To use, touch the item with the target vehicle and it will be automaticaly applied. This item can be purchased at the supermarket.


Contraband Items:

Cable Tie: The cable tie is a type of handcuff used to restrain players, usually for kidnapping. This tie also has the ability to gag and blind players, preventing them from being able to see or use voice chat.

Leash: The leash is a variant of the cable tie that allows players to forcefully pull constrained players with them. To enable/disable the leash, press your reload key while looking at the player.

Molotov: The Molotov is a throwable glass bottle that starts a fire wherever it lands. Players must have a roleplay-related reason to throw.

Bronze Money Printer: The bronze money printer is an illegal item that can produce usable in-game money. Bronze is the lowest quality printer out of the five, and can only be found while searching a dumpster. Printer stats: Prints $50 every 3 minutes

Silver Money Printer: The silver money printer is an illegal item that can produce usable in-game money. The silver printer can be purchased at the contraband NPC and can be inventoried. Printer stats: Prints $150 every 2 minutes.

Gold Money Printer: The gold money printer is an illegal item that can produce usable in-game money. The gold printer can be purchased at the contraband NPC but cannot be inventoried. Printer stats: Prints $250 every 1 minute.

Diamond Money Printer: The diamond money printer is an illegal item that can produce usable in-game money. The diamond printer can only be obtained by a random chance when you rob the police bank. Printer stats: Prints $350 every 1 minute.

Platinum Money Printer: The platinum money printer is an illegal item that can produce usable in-game money. The platinum printer can only be obtained by a random chance when you cash in a money bag stolen from a deposit box. Printer stats: Prints $425 every 1 minute.

Flashbang: The flashbang is a type of grenade that temporarily blinds all players that look in the direction of the explosion, useful for quickly escaping a dangerous situation.

Smoke Grenade: The smoke grenade is a type of grenade that emits a cloud of smoke where it lands, making visibility very low.

Car Evictor: The car evictor is a tool that allows players to quickly break into a vehicle while someone is driving it. To use, left click on a car door with a player inside to kick them out and unlock the door. Please note that this is not a lockpick, so if you are trying to steal a vehicle with no players inside, the car evictor will not work.

Meth Stove: The meth stove is a gas stove that allows players to cook meth with the required ingredients. To use, place a meth pot with the required ingredients in it on the top, and place stove gas on the side. The cooking process will then start automatically.

Meth Stove Gas: Stove gas is a gas canister used for fueling the meth and cocaine stoves. To use, touch the canister with the stove, and they will become welded to eachother, meaning the stove is ready to use.

Meth Pot: The meth pot is a pot used to collect all ingredients used for making meth. To use, place the required ingredients on the pot, and place the pot on a fueled stove for it to start cooking. Comes in consumable and selling variants.

Chloride: Chloride is one of the required ingredients for making meth. To use, touch the ingredient with a meth pot.

Sodium: Sodium is one of the required ingredients for making meth. To use, touch the ingredient with a meth pot.

Weed Plant: The weed plant is a plant that grows weed. To use, place down and wait for the plant to grow. Once it has fully grown, press your use key to harvest the drug. Please note that this item only outputs consumable weed. You must use the purifier to convert it into marketable.

Gas Can: The gas can is a weapon that can be used to pour gas over a certain area. To pour gas, hold your primary fire. To ignite the gas, make sure you are close to the poured gas and press your secondary fire.

Market-Grade Weed/Meth: Market-grade drugs cannot be consumed but can be sold to the drug NPC for a certain amount of money.

Consumable-Grade Weed/Meth: Consumable-grade drugs cannot be sold and give you boosts such as increased health or armor, but can start to affect your health if taken too much.

One-Time-Use Taser: This is a special type of taser that works the same as the ones used by the police, but can only be used one time before being removed.

Disguise Kit: The disguise kit is used to change the user's playermodel to that of a certain job's playermodel. To use, left click while the SWEP is equipped, then press the number associated with the job you want to disguise as.


Farming Items:

Seed: Many different seeds can be purchased at the NPC shop, and planted anywhere. Once a plant has grown food, you can harvest the food to eat and restore health, or to sell for a profit.

Shipping Crate: The shipping crate is used for shipping grown crops to the farmer NPC to sell. To use, place the crate next to the farmer NPC and press your use key to recieve your profit.


Emergency Response Items:

Fire Extinguisher: The fire extinguisher is used for putting out fires, and has unlimited use.

Med Kit: The med kit is used to quickly heal players. You may heal players up to 150 HP if you are standing near an ambulance.

Narcan: Narcan is used to cure any negative effects a player has from consuming too many drugs.


News Cameraman Items:

News TV: The news TV is used to watch a live feed from a news cameraman's camera. To turn it on and tune stations, simply press your use key on it.

News Camera: The news camera is used for broadcasting a live feed to the news TV. This item is only available for the news cameraman. To use it, simply equip it and it will automatically start the broadcast.


Mining/Logging Items:

Log Cart: The log cart is simply used for holding logs.

Ore Cart: The ore cart is the same as the log cart, but holds ores instead of logs.

Wood Axe: The wood axe is used for chopping down trees.

Pickaxe: The pickaxe is used for mining ores and iron from rocks.

Ore Rocks: Ore rocks are rocks that, when mined with a pickaxe, produce ores that can be sold for a profit. They come in both small and large sizes and have a red health bar.

Iron Rocks: Iron rocks are rocks that, when mined with a pickaxe, produce iron that is used in crafting. They all come in large sizes and have a blue health bar.

Red/Green Trees: Red and green trees are able to be chopped down with an axe chopped down, they produce logs that can be used in crafting and can be sold for a profit. Red trees are very large and can easily be made out from a distance. Green trees are small and may be hard to distinguish from other map trees. Both of the trees' health bars are brown.


Crafting Table Items:

Please note that guns are not included with this list.

Frag Grenade: The frag grenade is a throwable explosive that deals a great amount of damage for anyone close the explosion.

Toy Turtle: The toy turtle is a decoration entity that simply hops around.

Lockpick: The lockpick is a tool used to silently break into locked building and vehicles. To use, walk up to either a door or vehicle and wait for the progress bar to finish.

Premium Lockpick: The premium lockpick is the same as the normal lockpick, except picking time is cut in half, picking creates no sound, and picking does not set off car alarms.

Suppressors Pack: The suppressors pack is a pack of suppressor weapon attachments. To use, press your use key on the box to claim the attachments, then press c while holding a compatible weapon to select the attachment.

Rifle Ammo Types: Rifle ammo types is a weapon attachment that allows you to change the type of ammo your weapon fires. See above to know how to equip the different ammo types. Please note that only rifles, smgs, and pistols can use this attachment.

Shotgun Ammo Types: Shotgun ammo types are similar to rifle ammo types but only support shotguns and no other weapons. See above to know how to equip the different ammo types.

Wrench: Wrenches are similar to springs but are more expensive to craft and are used less often in crafting recipies.

Timed C4: The timed C4 is a plastic explosive with a timer. The timer can range from 0 seconds to 10 minutes.

SLAM Remote Explosive: The SLAM is an explosive that can be detonated via a remote, or placed on a wall and used as a tripmine.

Long Range Scopes: This is a pack of long range scope attachments, mainly for use on rifles.

40mm Grenade Launcher Attachment: This attachment is used on certain assault rifles to launch a 40mm smoke or frag grenade.

Car Bomb: The car bomb is a special type of explosive used specifically on vehicles. When using this, keep in mind that the police have special tools for detecting and defusing these bombs.


F4 Menu Items:

Signs: Signs come in various sizes and patterns, they can be used to display text and/or images. Normal signs display stationary images or text, ticker signs display moving text.

Small Sign: The small sign is a clipboard that allows you to edit and save text. You are able to transport this in your inventory, although it will not save the text when the server restarts. If you want to save text, dupe the clipboard. This item can be spawned from the spawn menu under the entities tab.

Blue Book: The blue book is a book that allows you to edit and save text. You are able to transport this in your inventory, although it will not save the text when the server restarts. If you want to save text, dupe the book. This item can be spawned from the spawn menu under the entities tab.


Supermarket Items:

Please note that guns and farming items are not included in this list.

Media Player TV: This item has the ability to play videos and music through a number of different websites. To play music or a video, press your use key on the TV, then aim at the TV and hold C. You will then be given a menu to add videos. This item is available for purchase at the supermarket and has inventory support.

Civilian Fire Extinguisher: The civilian fire extinguisher is similar to the regular one used by emergency responders, however, this one can be bought by any job and only lasts 60 seconds after being used.

Tire Repair Tool: The tire repair tool is used for repairing popped tires. To use, go up to a vehicle and left click with the tool equipped. This item is available for purchase at the supermarket.

Crowbar: The crowbar is a tool used for prying ATMs open, it can also be swung like a bat.

Crafting Table: The crafting table is used for crafting other items such as weapons and tools. See the crafting table items section of this page to see what items are available to craft.

25% Vehicle Health: The vehicle heal kit is used for repairing vehicles that have taken damage. To use, touch the kit with a damaged vehicle. Please note that this only restores 25% of a vehicle's health back; if you need a full repair, contact a tow truck driver.

Slot Machine: The slot machine is mainly used in casinos to have players pay to have a chance to win a lot more cash. To use, press your use key on the machine; it will deduct $100 from you and begin to spin. You then have the chance to wi from $5 to $20,000.

One-Time-Use Health Kit: This is a special type of health kit that fully restores a player's health, at the cost of only having one use.

Big Analog Clock: Analog clock that displays the time reported by the weather system.

Bedside Digital Clock: Small digital clock that displays the time reported by the weather system in red text.

Holographic Clock: Small digital clock that displays the time reported by the weather system in blue text floating above the clock.

Large Metal Detector: Alternative weapon detector players can use instead of the handheld one. Detects SMGs, shotguns, assault rifles, explosives, as well as weapons in a player's inventory. Doesn't require a player to operate.

DJ Radio: Used to play music selected from DJ radio controllers.

DJ Controller: Used to broadcast soundcloud and youtube audio to radios and speakers.

DJ Speaker: Used to play music selected from DJ radio controllers, plays at a wider and adjustable distance, unlike the radio.

Business Sign: The business sign is a simple sign that displays open and closed. To use, press your use key on it.

Life Alert: Alerts police and EMS if your location when you type !alert in chat if you have this equipped.


Cook Items:

Pizza Oven: Used to cook pizzas that have been put together.

Pizza Fridge: Used to buy ingredients for making pizza. Dough is the pizza base, everything else is a topping.

Cooking Stove: The cooking stove is used to cook any fish you have caught as a cook. To cook fish, press your use key on it, a pot will appear to indicate that your fish are cooking. The fish will appear on the stove once they are done.
