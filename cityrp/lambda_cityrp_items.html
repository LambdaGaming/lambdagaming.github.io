CityRP Server Items

Note: This is a list of all items that can be bought/found within the RP. This does not include tools from the spawn menu or guns. Press Ctrl+F if you're looking for a specific item.

Public Items:

ATM:
Used to deposit and withdraw money, as well as taking out loans. Two ATMs can be found in the bank of each map.

Banker NPC:
Used to cash-in checks and money bags. To use, press your use key on a check or money bag that is in range of the NPC.

Blueprint Dumpster:
Dumpster colored gray that can be found at the gas station closest to spawn on every map. Pressing your use key on it will deduct $1,200 from your wallet and spawn a tier 1 blueprint next to the dumpster.

Breakout NPC:
Used by criminals who have been arrested to break out of jail. To use, someone has to first break you out of your cell. You can then go to the police station front desk and press your use key on the NPC. A timer will then start for your release and the police will be notified.

Courier NPC:
Used to deliver packages to other players. You will receive $800 every time you deliver a package. Packages can be stolen by killing a player who has one. Stolen packages can be sold to the banker NPC for $2,000.

Contraband NPC:
Sells a variety of items that are illegal by default such as weed plants and money printers. The mayor can decide for each item whether it should be illegal or not.

Deposit Box Van:
White van located in certain parking lots or gas stations of every map that's used to cash-in stolen deposit box money bags. To cash a bag in, touch it with the van. After cashing in a bag, there is a 30% chance you will be rewarded with a platinum money printer.

Drug NPC:
Used to sell weed, meth, and cocaine. To sell your drugs, press your use key on a drug that is in range of the NPC. Government and police jobs have to wait 30 seconds before the money is dropped.

Dumpster:
Used to find potentially valuable items. Valuable items have a 40% chance of spawning, and can range from simple fish bait to a pistol. Once a dumpster is used, a 2 minute cooldown will be in effect before it can be searched again.

Farm NPC:
Used to sell farm food. You must place your food in a shipping crate, place the crate near the NPC, and press your use key on the crate for the NPC to accept the items.

Fishing NPC:
Used to purchase fishing equipment such as bait, rods, and cages. Once you have caught some fish, you can go back to the NPC to sell them for a profit.

Government Bank Vault:
Large vault that can hold up to $20,000. To rob, press your use key on the money pallet and money bags will spawn in front of you. The police will automatically be alerted to the robbery as soon as it starts. You can keep robbing until the pallet amount reaches zero. After you are done robbing, you must take the bags to the banker NPC to get the cash.

Health NPC:
Used to restore heath and/or armor for a fee. Full health restoration costs $100, a one-time-use health kit costs $200, and full armor restoration costs $800.

Iron Refiner:
Used to refine craftable items back into iron. To use, touch a craftable item with the refiner and it will begin refining. The refining time varies depending on how much the item is worth. The refiner can only refine one item at a time.

Item Bank:
Storage entity located in the bank of each map that allows players to to store items from their inventories. To use, go up to it and press your use key, you can then drag items from your inventory into the bank.

Mayor Election NPC:
Used to enter the mayor elections. Once at least two players have entered the election, it will begin after 60 seconds. Elections will not start if there is already a mayor in office.

Police Bank Vault:
Small vault that can hold up to $5,000. To rob, press your use key on the money pile and wait for the timer to finish. The police will automatically be alerted to the robbery as soon as it starts, and leaving the area will cause the robbery to fail.

Purifier:
Used to purify consumable weed into marketable weed, and to purify raw cocaine into pure cocaine. To use, touch consumable weed or raw cocaine with the purifier. Each consumable weed will take 45 seconds to purify, and each raw cocaine will take 10 minutes. Users can stop the cocaine purifying process early to get a better purity by pressing their use key on the purifier. Once the item is done purifying, it will spawn next to the purifier.

Rock and Tree Grinders:
Accepts cartloads of logs or ores. To use, fill a rock or wood cart with the appropriate material, and take it to the appropriate grinder. Grinding time and cash earned depends on the density of the ores or logs.

Shop NPC:
Sells a wide variety of items such as low-grade weapons, vehicle repair tools, and media players. The NPC only sells items that are legal by default, although some items that are sold can be used illegally. Items sold by this NPC can be affected by the sales tax set by the mayor.

Smuggle Buy NPC:
Used by criminals to buy illegal items to be shipped to a smuggle sell NPC. Items that can be smuggled vary in price, but the more expensive the item is, the higher the reward and risk.

Smuggle Deliver NPC:
Used by criminals to sell illegal items that were bought from the smuggle buy NPC. To use, press your use key on the NPC, you will then receive payment for the smuggle and your shipping truck will be removed.

Soda Machine:
Used to restore small amounts of health. To use one, go up to it and press your use key. A soda will come out and you will automatically be deducted 30 dollars. To gain health, press your use key on the soda once it falls out of the machine.

Truck NPC:
Used to rent trucks and trailers for transporting items. Purchasing a truck or trailer will spawn it in the nearest open road or parking lot.

Vehicle NPC:
Used to buy, sell, and store vehicles. When selling a vehicle, you will receive 75% of what you bought it for. Spawning a new vehicle from this NPC will store your old one, so be aware of the rules surrounding that.

Law Enforcement Items:

Speed Radar:
Used to track the speed of a vehicle. To place it, aim close to the ground where you want it placed and left click. To remove it, walk up to it and right click. The speed trap will display the fastest speed it's detected so far.

Handheld Speed Radar:
Concealed version of the speed radar that only undercover officers have access to for more secretive traffic enforcement. Left click while looking at a vehicle to get a speed reading.

Police Handcuffs:
Used for restraining a player without arresting them. To cuff a player, left click. To uncuff a player, press your use key on the target player.

Police Leash:
Used by SWAT units to restrain a player without arresting them. The leash feature allows units to drag players who might otherwise run away with normal handcuffs. The leash has slightly less strength than the police handcuffs. To enable/disable the leash, press your reload key while looking at the player.

Police Badge:
Used by all law enforcement officials to make sure they are official officers and not fakes.

Spike Strip:
Used to stop a vehicle by popping it's tires as the vehicle runs over the strip. Vehicles with popped tires won't stop immediately after running over the strip, however. They will continue to move for a few seconds before slowing down enough to be stopped by another force. If a spike strip is left on the ground for more than 10 minutes, it will be auto removed and given back to the officer who placed it.

Taser:
Used to stun someone and temporarily stop them from moving. Left click to fire, please note that you must be fairly close to the suspect for the taser to hit and the taser takes 3 seconds to charge up after pulling the trigger. A single-use version of the taser is available at the contraband NPC for non-law enforcement players.

Handheld Metal Detector:
Used to quickly check for weapons without having the need to set up a normal metal detector. Left click to use, right click to toggle power. Please note that this item won't detect explosives or weapons in a players inventory.

Battering Ram:
Used to break through doors after receiving a search warrant. To use, right click to hold it up into position, left click in front of a door to force it open.

Arrest/Unarrest Stick:
Used to arrest players after they are cuffed. Left click to arrest/unarrest a handcuffed player, right click to change type of stick. When you left click, a menu will open and you can decide how long the arrested player stays in jail for.

Confiscation Tool:
Used to take weapons and non-default tools from a player. Once you have confiscated items from a player, you can either give them back at a later time or destroy the items. While you are holding a player's items, you cannot confiscate from another player.

Car Bomb Checker: Used to check for and defuse car bombs. Left click to check the target vehicle for car bombs, and right click to defuse and remove the bomb if the target vehicle has one.

Tow Truck Driver Items:

Car Bomb Protection:
Used to prevent players from planting bombs on vehicles that have this equipped. To use, touch the item with the target vehicle and it will be automatically applied.

Brake Release Tool:
Used to release the parking brakes of locked vehicles so they can be safely towed. To use, walk up to a vehicle and left click.

Repair Tool:
Used by tow truck drivers to fix up broken vehicles. Left click repairs the target vehicle by 10 HP, right click repairs the target vehicle's tires.

Tow SWEP:
Used for attaching a tow truck to another car. To use, back your tow truck up to the very front of the target vehicle, and left click while looking at the tow truck.

Banker Items:

Check Machine:
Used by the banker to spawn in checks for emergency situations such as a hostage negotiation. To use, press your use key on the machine and you will be asked to confirm your choice to spawn a check. Once the request has been confirmed, a check will spawn in front of the machine. Note that this item subtracts money from the government bank when used.

Bagged Deposit Box Money:
Bag of money that was stolen from a player's deposit box. To cash one in, touch it with the deposit box van.

Deposit Box:
Used by the banker to store other players' money. Over time, both the banker and the player who deposited the money will receive money from interest. If the banker disconnects or changes jobs while money is still in a deposit box, that money will be refunded back to it's owner. This item can be purchased by the banker through the F4 menu.

Deposit Box Computer:
Displays details about a player's deposit box such as the original deposited amount, the current amount, and the current interest rate. This item can be purchased by the banker through the F4 menu.

Contraband Items:

Bronze Money Printer:
Used to produce usable in-game money. Bronze is the lowest quality printer out of the five, and can only be found while searching a dumpster. Printer stats: Prints $50 every 3 minutes

Cable Tie:
Used to restrain players, usually for kidnapping. This tie also has the ability to gag and blind players, preventing them from being able to see or use voice chat.

Car Evictor:
Used to quickly break into a vehicle while someone is driving it. To use, left click on a car door with a player inside to kick them out and unlock the door.

Chloride:
One of the required ingredients for making meth. To use, touch the ingredient with a meth pot.

Coca Plant:
Comes in consumable and marketable variants. Outputs raw cocaine after spending a certain amount of time under a heat lamp. The amount of time required to grow depends on the variant of the plant.

Consumable-Grade Weed/Meth/Cocaine:
Consumable-grade drugs cannot be sold and give you boosts such as increased health or armor, but can start to affect your health if taken too much.

Contraband Package:
Similar to the courier package, except it contains an unknown illegal item. Once you buy it from the contraband NPC, you will get a message saying what NPC to deliver the package to. Once the package is near the target NPC, press your use key on it to deliver the package and get a tier 2 blueprint reward. Packages can be stolen by pressing your use key on a package that doesn't belong to you.

Diamond Money Printer:
Used to produce usable in-game money. The diamond printer can only be obtained by a random chance when you rob the police bank. Printer stats: Prints $350 every 1 minute.

Disguise Kit:
Used to change the users playermodel to that of a certain jobs playermodel. To use, left click while the SWEP is equipped, then press the number associated with the job you want to disguise as. Note that if you take damage while disguised, the disguise will be removed.

Flashbang:
Type of grenade that temporarily blinds all players that look in the direction of the explosion, useful for quickly escaping a dangerous situation.

Gas Can:
Used to pour gas over a certain area. To pour gas, hold your primary fire. To ignite the gas, make sure you are close to the poured gas and press your secondary fire.

Gold Money Printer:
Used to produce usable in-game money. The gold printer can be purchased at the contraband NPC but cannot be inventoried. Printer stats: Prints $250 every 1 minute.

Hacking Station:
Used to hack and view stats of all drones that are currently spawned in the map. To hack, press your use key on the console type 'hack'. You will be asked to enter a password. After you enter the password it will list the ID of all of the available drones.

Heat Lamp:
Used to provide heat to coca plants so they grow. Has a 20% chance of catching fire every 5 minutes while turned on.

Leash:
Variant of the cable tie that allows players to forcefully pull constrained players with them. To enable/disable the leash, press your reload key while looking at the player.

Market-Grade Weed/Meth/Cocaine:
Market-grade drugs cannot be consumed but can be sold to the drug NPC for a certain amount of money.

Meth Stove:
Used to cook meth. To use, place a meth pot with the required ingredients in it on the top, and place stove gas on the side. The cooking process will then start automatically.

Meth Stove Gas:
Used for fueling the meth stove. To use, touch the canister with the stove, and they will become welded to each other, meaning the stove is ready to use. If needed, you can press your use key on the canister to detach it from the stove.

Meth Pot:
Used to collect all ingredients used for making meth. To use, place the required ingredients on the pot, and place the pot on a fueled stove for it to start cooking. Comes in consumable and selling variants.

Molotov:
Throwable glass bottle that starts a fire wherever it lands.

One-Time-Use Taser:
Special type of taser that works the same as the ones used by the police, but can only be used one time before being removed.

Platinum Money Printer:
Used to produce usable in-game money. The platinum printer can only be obtained by a random chance when you cash in a money bag stolen from a deposit box. Printer stats: Prints $425 every 1 minute.

Raw Cocaine:
Raw form of cocaine that is output by the coca plant. Insert it into the purifier to turn it in to pure cocaine. See the purifier description for more details.

Silver Money Printer:
Used to produce usable in-game money. The silver printer can be purchased at the contraband NPC and can be inventoried. Printer stats: Prints $150 every 2 minutes.

Smoke Grenade:
Emits a cloud of smoke where it lands, making visibility very low.

Sodium:
One of the required ingredients for making meth. To use, touch the ingredient with a meth pot.

Weed Plant:
Used to grow weed. To use, place down and wait for the plant to grow. Once it has fully grown, press your use key to harvest the drug. Please note that this item only outputs consumable weed. You must use the purifier to convert it into marketable.

Farming Items:

Seed:
Many different seeds can be purchased at the shop NPC and planted anywhere. Once a plant has grown food, you can harvest the food to eat and restore health, or to sell for a profit.

Shipping Crate:
Used for transporting grown crops to the farmer NPC to sell. To use, place the crate next to the farmer NPC and press your use key to receive your profit.

Emergency Response Items:

Defibrillator:
Used to revive players who have died and haven't respawned yet. To use, press your primary fire while near a players corpse.

Fire Extinguisher:
Used for putting out fires. This version has unlimited use.

Med Kit:
Used to quickly heal players. You may heal players up to 150 HP if you are standing near an ambulance.

Narcan:
Used to cure any negative effects a player has from overdosing on drugs.

News Cameraman Items:

News Camera:
Used for broadcasting a live feed to the news TV. To use it, simply equip it and it will automatically start the broadcast. You can also edit the ticker text that appears on the TVs by right clicking.

News TV:
Used to watch a live feed from a news camera.

Mining/Logging Items:

Axe:
Used for chopping down trees for logs.

Iron Rocks:
Rocks that, when mined with a pickaxe, produce iron that can be used in crafting. They all come in large sizes and have a blue health bar.

Log Cart:
Used for holding up to 20 logs. Place a full log cart in a tree grinder to earn a profit.

Ore Cart:
Used for holding up to 20 ores. Place a full ore cart in an ore grinder to earn a profit.

Ore Rocks:
Rocks that, when mined with a pickaxe, produce ores that can be sold for a profit. They come in both small and large sizes and have a red health bar.

Pickaxe:
Used for mining ores and iron from rocks.

Red/Green Trees:
Trees that, when chopped with an axe, produce logs that can be used in crafting or sold for a profit. Red trees are very large and can easily be made out from a distance. Green trees are small and may be hard to distinguish from other map trees. Both of the trees' health bars are brown.

Crafting Table Items:

Please note that guns are not included with this list.

40mm Grenade Launcher Attachment:
Used on certain assault rifles to launch a 40mm smoke or frag grenade.

Car Bomb:
Special type of explosive used specifically on vehicles. When using this, keep in mind that the police have a special tool for detecting and defusing these bombs.

Frag Grenade:
Throwable explosive that deals a great amount of damage for anyone nearby by launching fragments of it's metal shell outward.

Lockpick:
Used to silently break into locked building and vehicles. To use, walk up to a door or vehicle, press your primary fire, and wait for the progress bar to finish.

Long Range Scopes:
Pack of long range scope attachments, mainly used on rifles.

Nano Drone:
Small drone that is near impossible to detect without a hacking station. It doesn't emit sound but also doesn't display as many stats as the spy drones.

Premium Lockpick:
Same as the normal lockpick, except picking time is cut in half, it creates no sound, and does not set off car alarms.

Rifle Ammo Types:
Weapon attachment that allows you to change the type of ammo your weapon fires. See above to know how to equip the different ammo types. Please note that only rifles, SMGs, and pistols can use this attachment.

RPG:
Rocket explosive that comes automatically with 3 rockets after being crafted. Once all 3 rockets have been fired, the RPG will remove itself from the player's weapon slot.

Shotgun Ammo Types:
Similar to rifle ammo types but only support shotguns. See above to know how to equip the different ammo types.

SLAM Remote Explosive:
Explosive that can be detonated via a remote from anywhere in the map. It can also be placed on a wall and used as a tripmine.

Spider Drone:
Has all of the abilities as a spy drone but crawls instead of flies. It can also scale walls and walk on ceilings.

Suppressors Pack:
Pack of suppressor weapon attachments. To use, press your use key on the box to claim the attachments, then press c while holding a compatible weapon to select the attachment.

Timed C4:
Plastic explosive with a timer that does incredible amounts of damage. The timer can range from 0 seconds to 10 minutes.

Toy Turtle:
Hops around like a toy, can be configured to play a different sound at a different speed by pressing your use key on it.

Wrench:
Used in crafting recipes, usually advanced weapons.

Misc. Items:

Crafting Blueprint:
Used as a crafting item in certain recipes. They come in 3 tiers, which each tier containing better weapons. They can be obtained through various things such as using the blueprint dumpster, delivering a contraband package, completing a random event, or robbing the bank. All blueprints have a total of 3 uses before being depleted.

Small Sign/Blue Book:
Clipboard/book that allows you to edit and save text. You are able to transport this in your inventory, although it will not save the text when the server restarts. If you want to save text, dupe the clipboard. Available in the F4 menu.

Tip Jar:
Used to give money to a person without being physically near them. Available in the F4 menu.

Supermarket Items:

Please note that guns, farming items, and any other items that were already listed are not included in this list.

30% Vehicle Health:
Used for repairing vehicles that have taken damage. To use, touch the kit with a damaged vehicle. Please note that this only restores 30% of a vehicle's health back; if you need a full repair, contact a tow truck driver.

Announcement Microphone:
Talk while this is equipped to broadcast your voice to announcement speakers. Left click to change the channel.

Announcement Speakers:
Used alongside the announcement microphone to broadcast your voice. Supports up to 5 channels.

Business Sign:
Simple sign that displays the text 'Open' or 'Closed'. To change its status, press your use key on it.

Camera Drone:
Simple drone with a camera attached to it. Note that all drones automatically come with a remote control.

Civilian Fire Extinguisher:
Similar to the regular one used by emergency responders, but can be bought by any job and has limited ammo.

Crowbar:
Used for prying ATMs open, it can also be swung like a bat.

Crafting Table:
Used for crafting other items such as weapons and tools. See the crafting table items section of this page to see what items are available to craft. Note that most items in the crafting table now require a blueprint to be able to be crafted.

DJ Radio:
Used to play music selected from DJ radio controllers.

DJ Controller:
Used to broadcast Soundcloud and Youtube audio to radios and speakers.

DJ Speaker:
Used to play music selected from DJ radio controllers, plays at a wider and adjustable distance, unlike the radio.

Large Metal Detector:
Alternate weapon detector players can use instead of the handheld one. Detects SMGs, shotguns, assault rifles, explosives, as well as weapons in a player's inventory. Doesn't require a player to operate.

Life Alert:
Alerts police and EMS if your location when you type !alert in chat if you have it equipped. Press your use key on it to equip it.

Media Player TV:
Plays videos and music through a number of different websites. To play music or a video, press your use key on the TV, then aim at the TV and hold C. You will then be given a menu to add videos.

One-Time-Use Health Kit:
Special type of health kit that fully restores a player's health, at the cost of only having one use before removing itself.

Slot Machine:
Used in casinos to have players pay to have a chance to win a lot more cash. To use, press your use key on the machine; it will deduct $100 from you and begin to spin. You then have the chance to win from $5 to $20,000.

Spy Drone:
Similar to the camera drone but can detect entities.

Fuel Can:
Used to fill up a car's fuel tank. It can fill up to 75% of the vehicle's capacity. To use, touch it with a vehicle that has less than 100% fuel.

Cook Items:

Alcohol Case:
Item that the keg spawns when it's done brewing an alcoholic drink. Each case contains 6 individual bottles that can be consumed to restore up to 40 hunger depending on the type of drink.

Cooked Fish:
Output of the cooking stove. Can be eaten to restore 50 health and 20 hunger.

Cooking Stove:
Used to cook any fish you have caught as a cook. To cook fish, press your use key on it. A pot will appear to indicate that your fish are cooking. The fish will appear on the stove once they are done.

Keg:
Used to create alcoholic drinks by placing a keg fruit inside and letting it brew for 100 seconds.

Keg Fruits:
Various fruits that can be placed in a keg to create specific alcoholic drinks.

Pizza Fridge:
Used to buy ingredients for making pizza. Dough is the pizza base, everything else is a topping.

Pizza Oven:
Cooks completed pizzas. It will automatically start running once you put a pizza in one of the slots.

Microwave:
Used to quickly prepare food. You can get food from it by pressing your use key on it, or you can let your customers use it while you get a small profit.
