NPC & Item Locations

Note: These descriptions were made with the assumption that you have a general idea of the layouts for each map. If you are lost and have no clue where you are, contact an admin and you will be shown around.

rp_rockford_v2b:

Accessory Vendor: Uptown, behind taco bell and the gas station, front desk of the clothing store.

Banker: Uptown, across from real estate building and supermarket, grassy area of mesa apartments near the benches.

Car Dealer: Main city, across from bank and fire station, front desk of GM car dealership. Vehicles spawn on the left side of the dealership in front of the garage door.

Contraband Dealer: Uptown, across from bus station and mesa apartments, alleyway behind real estate building and supermarket.

Courier NPC: Uptown, next to taco bell and supermarket, across from bus station, standing outside the gas station store entrance.

Drug Dealer: Outskirts, down the road from yellow warehouse and up the road from rock and tree grinders, left side of the gas station.

Farmer: Main city, next to city hall and fire station, behind police department in the grass.

Firefighter: Main city, across from car dealership, next to bank and police station, behind city hall, fire station front desk. Fire truck spawns inside the garage.

Fisherman: Outskirts, across from cabins and blue warehouse, at the end of the dock on the lake.

Government Bank Vault: Main city, across from car dealership, next to fire station, behind city hall, vault in the back of the bank.

Healer: Main city, across from city hall, hospital front desk.

Mayors Secretary: Main city, across from run-down apartments, next to police department and hospital, first floor of city hall, front desk.

Paramedic: Main city, across from city hall, hospital front desk. Ambulance spawns on the left side of the hospital inside the emergency bay.

Police Bank Vault: Main city, next to city hall and fire station, police station, first room on the right after you pass the elevator.

Police Secretary: Main city, next to city hall and fire station, front desk of police department. Vehicles spawn in the PD parking lot.

Purifier: Main city, next to hospital and car dealer, grassy hill.

Rock and Tree Grinders: Outskirts, down the road from the outskirts house and gas station, dead end road in the corner of the map.

Smuggle Buyer: Outskirts, across from ravine, grassy area to the left of the road leading to suburbs.

Smuggle Seller: Main city, across from city hall, next to hospital, in between the two run-down apartment buildings. The flat bed truck spawns in the city hall parking lot, parking space on the right, closest to the road.

Supermarket: Uptown, behind real estate building, next to gas station and mesa apartments, supermarket building, register closest to the door.

Tower: Main city, next to auto body shop, across from car dealership, front desk of towing building. Tow truck spawns in the gravel lot to the right of the main building.

Trucker: Uptown, next to gas station and movie theater, inside the transportation building.
