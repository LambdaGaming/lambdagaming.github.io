Why does my game randomly freeze and unfreeze?

This is usually caused by players spawning in new vehicles. Since the server usually doesn't use a precacher to load the models. This could also be the result of low memory on your PC, or a low-end processor taking the time to load resources.


How do I pick a job?

Press F4 to open the job menu. If you are playing on a laptop or Mac, you may have to hold down a function key while pressing the F4 key.


What are the rules for this server?

Rules and guidelines for this server can be found by pressing F1 in-game, or by going to them through the site map of this website, which can be found to the left.


Why aren't there any criminal jobs?

To add an element of surprise to roleplay events, criminal abilities are put with the basic civilian jobs (Citizen, Tow Truck Driver, and News Cameraman). Some illegal things that civilians can do are stealing cars, mugging players for money, kidnaping, hostage taking, money printing, drug making, and bank robbing.


How do I make money?

There are plenty of ways (both legal and illegal) to make money. Some legal things to make money on are farming, mining, logging, running a business, tow truck driving (separate job), cooking (separate job), making a news station (separate job), and being hired as a detective (separate job).
Some illegal things to make money on are being hired as a hitman, mugging, money printing, drug making, stealing from ATMs, and bank robbing.


Why are there error signs and missing textures everywhere?

You are most likely missing some required addons. To get them, contact an admin for the link to the server's addon collection. If you are still seeing missing textures and errors after you download the addons and restart your game, you are most likely missing content from one or more source games, such as Counter-Strike:Source or Half-Life 2 Episode 2, which can be bought in the Steam store and then mounted onto Garry's Mod.


Do I need to announce my actions such as robbing the bank?

Nope! It is up to civilians to report crime as they see it go down. Higher-scale crimes such as robbing the bank have automated systems to alert police that the robbery is taking place.


Why does it take so long to load onto the server?

Server loading times for clients depend on a variety of things such as ping, client hardware, how many other clients are currently loading onto the server, and whether or not you have automatic downloads enabled. Usually clients don't connect all the way due to impatience from waiting for addons to download from the server. To speed up loading times and disable server downloads, go to your game options, click on the multiplayer tab, and you will see a drop down box for server download settings. UPDATE: Serverside download settings are no longer enabled, so players shouldn't be downloading addon files even if their download settings are enabled. If this is not the case please contact an admin or the server owner.
