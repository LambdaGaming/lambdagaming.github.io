Below is a list of default laws the city must go by if there is no mayor to change them. Everything listed has the ability to be changed by the mayor. Please note that these are IN-GAME ROLEPLAY LAWS!!! You will not be punished by admins for breaking these!

    Traffic:

  • Speeding, running red lights, running stop signs, and reckless driving is illegal.

  • If a player parks outside of a parking space, their vehicle must have it's hazard lights on.

  • Blocking traffic by any means without proper permission is illegal.

  • The use of turn signals is not required.

  • Driving lights are required to be on after 7:00 PM, and before 7:00 AM.

  • Armored vehicles (vehicles protected by props) are considered street-illegal.

  • Tow truck drivers are permitted to tow and impound illegally parked cars without consent from law enforcement officials.

  • Tow truck drivers are permitted to go past traffic, stop signs, and red lights if they are responding to a call.

  • Turning right at a red light is allowed.

  • Players must have a manifest when driving a truck with a loaded trailer.

  • Any player, no matter their job, is permitted to drive trucks as long as they have the required manifest stated above.

    • Weapons:

  • Open and conceal carry of weapons and/or tools on public property is illegal, except for knives, handguns, tire repair tools, and vehicle repair tools.

  • Gun licenses are not required.

  • Citizens who use explosives and/or fire to kill are to be considered terrorists.

  • Grenade launcher attachments are illegal, even if only used on private property.

  • Only rifles without full-auto and shotguns are permitted to be used in hunting.

    • Rights:

  • Anyone is permitted to join in a parade.

  • Citizens are not required to watch the news or listen to radio.

  • Players are permitted to choose any job they want to be.

  • Players can live anywhere they'd like as long as it doesn't go against other laws.

  • Citizens can run any business they'd like as long as it doesn't go against other laws.

  • Everyone has a right to free speech.

  • KOS zones are allowed to be placed by any player no matter what job they are.

  • News cameramen are permitted to record anyone without consent, but need permission to record on private property.

  • Profanity in public is legal.

  • Everyone has a right to any religion.

    • Contraband:

  • A license is required to sell alcohol and wine.

  • Money printers and drugs are illegal.

  • Items used to make drugs are illegal.

  • Money printer upgrades and coolant are illegal, printer paper is legal.

  • Smuggling is illegal.

  • Drugs cannot be used for medical purposes.

  • Slot machines are illegal if they are not used in official casinos.

    • Property:

  • A permit to build is not required on private property, but is required on public property.

  • Trespassing on unowned properties is legal. Trespassing on owned and government properties (PD, city hall office, declared government houses) is illegal.

  • Theft and robbery is highly illegal.

  • Dumpster diving is legal.

  • Detectives are permitted to enter private property and search players without permission ONLY if they are investigating.

  • Players cannot spawn in items that other players are selling, meaning if a player is selling chairs at their store, other players must buy from the shop and cannot spawn in chairs. Items that break this law are to be considered illegal imports.

    • Court/Arrests/Law Enforcement:

  • Citizen's arrest is legal as long as there is at least one active law enforcement officer in the city.

  • Anyone who has been arrested is permitted to still carry legal weapons after they are released, no matter what crime they committed.

  • Being corrupt as any job is illegal.

  • Attempting crimes but not actually doing it is also illegal.

  • The death sentence is only to be given to those who have killed players and have surrendered.

  • Police are permitted to arrest any player seen interacting with the drug NPC.

  • Police are NOT permitted to arrest players seen interacting with the contraband NPC.

  • Everyone has a right to court after they have been arrested.

  • Police must have sufficient evidence before making an arrest or charging a player.

  • Players are permitted to sue any player for any reason.

  • If a hitman kills someone, both the hitman and the person who hired him are to be held responsible for the death.

  • Players who declare themselves a terrorist through their job name or other in-game means are to be killed on sight.

  • Players who declare themselves a hitman through their job name or other in-game means are NOT to be killed on sight or arrested.

  • Police may weapon check players only if they feel that are a potential threat to themselves or others.

    • Citizen Well-Being:

  • Killing or injuring other players is illegal, unless it is proven to be self defense.

  • Harassment of any sort is illegal.

  • Players have a right to privacy only on their own property.

  • Discrimination of any kind is illegal.

  • Players who walk slowly are to be assumed to be under the influence of drugs.
