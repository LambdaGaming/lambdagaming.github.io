Default Laws

Below is a list of default laws the city must go by if there is no mayor to change them. Everything listed has the ability to be changed by the mayor, but the mayor is not restricted to just these laws. Please note that these are in-game roleplay laws. You won't get punished by admins for breaking these, though you may end up in legal trouble in the RP.


Traffic:

  • Running red lights, ignoring road signs, and reckless driving is illegal.

  • Blocking traffic by any means without proper permission is illegal.

  • Armored vehicles (vehicles protected by props) cannot be driven on public roads unless they are owned by the government.

  • Mechanics are permitted to impound or fine illegally parked cars without consent from law enforcement officials.

  • Mechanics, firefighters, and law enforcement officials are exempt from all traffic laws if they are responding to a call.

  • Players are permitted to drive semi-trucks and box trucks without a special license.

Weapons:

  • Open and concealed carry of weapons on public property is illegal, except for knives and handguns.

  • Gun licenses are not required.

  • Citizens who use explosives and/or fire to kill are to be considered terrorists.

  • Possession of explosive devices of any kind, even on private property, is illegal.

Rights:

  • Citizens can live anywhere and run any business they'd like as long as it doesn't break other laws.

  • Everyone has a right to freedom of speech, choice, and religion.

  • News cameramen are permitted to record anyone without consent, but need permission from the property owner to record while on private property.

  • Detectives have a right to keep client information private.

  • Players have a right to privacy only on their own property.

Contraband:

  • A license is required from the mayor or a law enforcement official to sell alcoholic beverages.

  • Money printers are illegal, but the paper they use is not.

  • Drugs and any items used to make drugs are illegal.

  • A license is required from the fire chief to use drugs for medical purposes.

  • Slot machines are illegal unless they are used on property that is advertised as a casino.

Property:

  • A permit to build is not required on private property, but is required on public property.

  • Trespassing on unowned property is legal. Trespassing on owned and restricted government properties (PD, city hall office, declared government houses) is illegal.

  • Destroying or stealing someone else's property is illegal.

  • Dumpster diving is legal.

  • Detectives are exempt from all property laws if the property they are investigating is related to a case.

Court/Arrests/Law Enforcement:

  • Civilians may not detain alleged criminals for any reason.

  • Players who have been arrested are still entitled to the same laws and rights as law-abiding players, no matter what crime they committed.

  • Being corrupt as any job is illegal.

  • Attempting to commit a crime is still illegal, even if there is no victim.

  • The death penalty is only to be given to those who have killed other players.

  • Police are permitted to arrest any player seen interacting with the drug NPC, but NOT the contraband NPC.

  • Everyone has a right to go to court to dispute charges after they have been arrested.

  • Police must have sufficient evidence before making an arrest or charging someone.

  • Players are permitted to sue any player for any reason.

  • If a hitman kills someone, both the hitman and the person who hired him are to be held responsible for the death.

  • Players who declare themselves a terrorist through their job name or other in-game means are to be killed on sight.

  • Police may weapon check players at any point for any reason.

  • Police need a warrant to search all private property, except vehicles.

Citizen Well-Being:

  • Killing or injuring other players is illegal, unless it is proven to be self defense.

  • Harassment of any sort is illegal.

  • Public intoxication is illegal. An intoxicated player counts as someone who has over 100 HP or someone who constantly walks slow.
