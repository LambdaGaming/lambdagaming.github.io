Default Laws

Below is a list of default laws the city must go by if there is no mayor to change them. Everything listed has the ability to be changed by the mayor. Please note that these are IN-GAME ROLEPLAY LAWS! You will NOT be punished by admins for breaking these!


Traffic:

  • Speeding, running red lights, running stop signs, and reckless driving is illegal.

  • Blocking traffic by any means without proper permission is illegal.

  • Armored vehicles (vehicles protected by props) cannot be driven on public roads.

  • Mechanics are permitted to impound or fine illegally parked cars without consent from law enforcement officials.

  • Mechanics, emergency responders, and law enforcement officials are exempt from traffic laws if they are responding to a call.

  • Turning right at a red light is allowed.

  • Players are permitted to drive semi-trucks and box trucks without a special license.

Weapons:

  • Open and concealed carry of weapons and/or tools on public property is illegal, except for knives, handguns, and vehicle repair tools.

  • Gun licenses are not required.

  • Citizens who use explosives and/or fire to kill are to be considered terrorists.

  • Possession of explosive devices of any kind, even on private property, is illegal.

Rights:

  • Anyone is permitted to join in a parade.

  • Citizens are not required to watch the news or listen to radio.

  • Players are permitted to choose any job they want to be.

  • Citizens can live anywhere and run any business they'd like as long as it doesn't break other laws.

  • Everyone has a right to free speech.

  • KOS zones are allowed to be placed by any player no matter what job they are.

  • News cameramen are permitted to record anyone without consent, but need permission from the property owner to record while on private property.

  • Everyone has a right to any religion.

  • Detectives have a right to keep client info private.

Contraband:

  • A license is required from the mayor or a law enforcement official to sell alcoholic beverages.

  • All money printers and drugs are illegal.

  • Items used to make drugs are illegal.

  • Money printer paper is legal.

  • Smuggling is illegal.

  • A license is required from an emergency responder to use drugs for medical purposes.

  • Slot machines are illegal if they are not used in buildings advertised as casinos.

Property:

  • A permit to build is not required on private property, but is required on public property.

  • Trespassing on unowned properties is legal. Trespassing on owned and restricted government properties (PD, city hall office, declared government houses) is illegal.

  • Theft and robbery is illegal.

  • Dumpster diving is legal.

  • Detectives are permitted to enter private property and search players without permission ONLY if they are investigating.

Court/Arrests/Law Enforcement:

  • Citizen's arrest is legal as long as the arrestees rights are being respected.

  • Players who have been arrested are still entitled to the same laws and rights as law-abiding players, no matter what crime they committed.

  • Being corrupt as any job is illegal.

  • Attempting crimes but not actually doing it is illegal.

  • The death penalty is only to be given to those who have killed other players.

  • Police are permitted to arrest any player seen interacting with the drug NPC.

  • Police are NOT permitted to arrest players seen interacting with the contraband NPC.

  • Everyone has a right to go to court to dispute charges after they have been arrested.

  • Police must have sufficient evidence before making an arrest or charging a player.

  • Players are permitted to sue any player for any reason.

  • If a hitman kills someone, both the hitman and the person who hired him are to be held responsible for the death.

  • Players who declare themselves a terrorist through their job name or other in-game means are to be killed on sight.

  • Police may weapon check players at any point for any reason.

  • Players who kill government or law enforcement officials are to receive the same treatment as any other player charged with murder.

Citizen Well-Being:

  • Killing or injuring other players is illegal, unless it is proven to be self defense.

  • Harassment of any sort is illegal.

  • Players have a right to privacy only on their own property.

  • Discrimination of any kind is illegal.

  • Public intoxication is illegal. An intoxicated player counts as someone who has over 100 HP or someone who constantly walks slow.
