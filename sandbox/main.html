Sandbox General Rules

Note: To see the specific rules regarding the server's basewars event, click here.

  1. Along with these rules, be sure to read the community-wide rules which can be found by clicking here.

  2. Don't build in the middle of high traffic areas. Examples of high traffic areas: Roads, footpaths, train tracks, spawn, any other areas that are regularly accessed by players and aren't regularly used for bases.

  3. Don't abuse buildmode. Example of abuse: noclipping to get into someone's base and killing them, or walking into an enemy base with god mode on to see the layout of the base without risk of dying.

  4. Don't damage vehicles until they are in-use so players have a fair chance to fight back.
