General Rules

Note: To see the specific rules regarding the server's basewars event, click here.

  1. Along with these rules, be sure to read the community-wide rules which can be found by clicking here.

  2. Don't build in the middle of high traffic areas. Examples of high traffic areas: Roads, footpaths, train tracks, spawn, and any other areas that are regularly accessed by players and aren't regularly used for bases.

  3. Don't use your noclip and god mode privileges for anything other than building. Breaking this rule will get them permanently revoked.

  4. Don't damage vehicles until they are in-use so players have a fair chance to fight back.
