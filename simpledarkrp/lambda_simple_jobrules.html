Mayor:

  • Don't abuse mayor commands or power in any way,

  • You cannot enforce laws that you set. The police force takes care of that.

  • You can be corrupt by making drugs or money printing, nothing more.

Police Chief:

  • You are always required to follow the mayors orders. If you think his orders are against server rules talk to him about it or contact an admin.

  • You can be corrupt by making drugs and money printing, nothing more.

Civil Protection:

  • You are always required to follow orders from the Police Chief or Mayor. If you think his orders are against server rules talk to him about it or contact an admin.

  • You can be corrupt by making drugs and money printing, nothing more.

  • If there isn't any Police Chief or Mayor on duty to give you orders, patrol the streets for crime.

Mob Boss:

  • Don't abuse your ability to earn money by freeing prisoners.

  • If you rob the bank, you do not have to annouce that you are doing so. Once the robbery has started the police are automatically notified.

Gangster:

  • You are required to always follow the mob boss's orders. If you feel the orders are against server rules, contact an admin.

  • If you rob the bank, you do not have to annouce that you are doing so. Once the robbery has started the police are automatically notified.

Gun Dealer:

  • You cannot deny service to anyone, unless a person has done something to negatively affect you.

Cook:

  • You cannot deny service to anyone, unless a person has done something to negatively affect you.

  • Keep your prices fair, otherwise you may not get much service.

Medic:

  • You can deny service only if the player hasn't paid already or if you have been ordered not to serve someone by the mayor or a police officer.

Civilian:


  • When running a store, you can sell anything for any price, it's all up to you.

  • When robbing a store, you can demand up to $500 or items worth up to that much.

  • When mugging a player, you can demand up to $200.

Hobo:

  • You can throw crap at people and ask for money, just don't get annoying with it, meaning don't follow one person all the time and constantly demand money.

  • You may build your own house but it must not block a road or entrance to a building, and it must follow the rules for keypads.
