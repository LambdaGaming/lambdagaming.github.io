About Us:

Lambda Gaming is a small (but growing) gaming community. We host non-profit game servers that are all unique in a way. Our servers are not 24/7, and all of our servers are currently hosted in the Eastern U.S. region. Below is a list of all of our servers.

Our Game Servers:

  • Garry's Mod Sandbox (Server from an old community, contains addons from other servers put together in a sandbox environment with dozens of maps.)

  • Garry's Mod Half-Life Universe Roleplay (This is our custom gamemode, based on the major events of the full Half-Life storyline.)

  • Garry's Mod CityRP (Plays like the default DarkRP gamemode but more advanced and takes place in a modern city environment. Rules are similar to those seen on Lawless RP servers.)

  • Garry's Mod Various Gamemodes Server (This server currently hosts the following gamemodes: Cops and Runners, DarkRP, Flood, Homicide, Jazztronauts, Melonbomber, Prop Hunt, Pirate Ship Wars, Pirates Vs Boners, Dropzone, Sacrifun, Sledbuild, TTT, Trash Compactor, and Zombie Survival. )

  • Garry's Mod Nazi Invasion RP (Focused on Nazis taking over areas such as Berlin, parts of France, and Stalingrad.)

  • Garry's Mod Chaos RP (An RP environment where random addons are constantly being added to make a unique RP experience. The server resets it's addons once clients or the server itself starts to lose performance due to the amount of addons.)
