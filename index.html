About Us:

We are a small gaming community mainly composed of a group of friends who used to play on a bunch of different Garry's Mod servers and came together to form this community. We host our own game servers, mostly consisting of Garry's Mod roleplay servers and various modded Minecraft servers. We do not run our servers 24/7. Below is a list of all of our servers. Please note that all servers have the potential to be password locked for testing or private sessions without an announcement. We now have an official Discord that you can join by clicking here.



Major Servers:

These servers are opened and updated more than the others.

  • Garry's Mod CityRP (Plays like the default DarkRP gamemode but more advanced and takes place in a modern city environment. Rules are similar to those seen on Lawless RP servers.)

  • Garry's Mod Half-Life Universe RP (This is our custom gamemode based on the major events of the full Half-Life storyline.)

  • Garry's Mod Sandbox (Server from an old community, contains addons from other servers put together in a sandbox environment with dozens of maps.)

  • Garry's Mod Various Gamemodes Server (This server currently hosts the following gamemodes: Cops and Runners, Flood, Homicide, Jazztronauts, Prop Hunt, Pirate Ship Wars Remix, Pirates Vs Boners, Dropzone, Sledbuild, Stop it Slender, TTT, Trash Compactor, and Zombie Survival. )

  • Garry's Mod Zombie RP (WIP, going to be sort of a mix between CityRP and the Zombie Survival gamemode.)

Private Servers:

Only moderators and admins are allowed to join these servers most of the time. Community members may be allowed to join under certain circumstances.

  • Minecraft Vanilla (Always runs the most up-to-date version of the game, not including snapshots.)

  • Minecraft Modded (Normal survival with basic rules, runs with around 50 mods on version 1.12.2.)

  • Minecraft Modded Unbalanced (Normal survival with little rules. Runs with around 60 OP mods that were rejected from the other servers. Runs on version 1.12.2. Only runs on beefy PCs!)

  • Minecraft Modded City Build (The main goal of this server is to cooperatively build a giant city using various mods to make the process go faster and look nicer. Runs on version 1.12.2.)

  • Minecraft Modded Earth (Runs the 1:1 scale Earth mod along with a few other mods. Players are allowed to use creative mode and teleport to build their bases but if they want to go to war with another player they can't use any of those features.)

  • Minecraft Modded RLCraft (Runs the most recent version of the RLCraft modpack. Not recommended for people with low amounts of patience.)

  • SCP: Secret Laboratory (Frequently updated with different mods.)

  • Broke Protocol: Online City RPG

  • Sven Co-Op

  • Killing Floor 2
