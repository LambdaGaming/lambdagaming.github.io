About Us:

We are a small gaming community mainly composed of a group of friends who used to play on a bunch of different Garry's Mod servers and came together to form this community. We host our own game servers, mostly consisting of Garry's Mod roleplay servers and various modded Minecraft servers. We do not run our servers 24/7. Below is a list of all of our servers. Please note that all servers have the potential to be password locked for testing or private sessions without an announcement. We now have an official Discord that you can join by clicking here.



Our Game Servers:

  • Garry's Mod Sandbox (Server from an old community, contains addons from other servers put together in a sandbox environment with dozens of maps.)

  • Garry's Mod Half-Life Universe Roleplay (This is our custom gamemode based on the major events of the full Half-Life storyline.)

  • Garry's Mod CityRP (Plays like the default DarkRP gamemode but more advanced and takes place in a modern city environment. Rules are similar to those seen on Lawless RP servers.)

  • Garry's Mod Various Gamemodes Server (This server currently hosts the following gamemodes: Cops and Runners, DarkRP, Flood, Homicide, Jazztronauts, Prop Hunt, Pirate Ship Wars Remix, Pirates Vs Boners, Dropzone, Sledbuild, Stop it Slender, TTT, Trash Compactor, and Zombie Survival. )

  • Garry's Mod Nazi Invasion RP (Focused on Nazis taking over areas such as Berlin, parts of France, and Stalingrad.)

  • Half-Life Deathmatch: Source

  • Sven Co-Op

  • Half-Life Deathmatch (Includes custom maps.)

  • Half-Life 2 Deathmatch

  • Ricochet (Includes custom maps.)

  • Team Fortress Classic

  • Team Fortress 2 (Includes a few of the most popular CP maps.)

  • Counter-Strike 1.6

  • Half-Life: Opposing Force Deathmatch

  • Insurgency (Currently only hosting default maps.)

  • Reign of Kings

  • Broke Protocol: Online City RPG (Currently whitelisted to community members only, admins have priority.)

  • Minecraft Vanilla (Limited player slots, community admins only. Always runs the most up-to-date version of the game.)

  • Minecraft Modded (Limited player slots, community admins only. Runs with around 50 mods on version 1.12.2.)

  • Minecraft Modded Unbalanced (Limited player slots, community admins only. Runs with around 60 OP mods that were rejected from the other server. Runs on version 1.12.2. Only runs on beefy PCs!)

  • Minecraft Modded City Build (Limited player slots, community admins only. The main goal of this server is to cooperatively build a giant city using various mods to make the process go faster and look nicer. Runs on version 1.12.2.)

  • Killing Floor 2 (Limited player slots, community members only, admins have priority.)
